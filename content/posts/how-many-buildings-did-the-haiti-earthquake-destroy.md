What countries were affected by the 2010 Haiti earthquake?
----------------------------------------------------------

2010 Haiti earthquake, large-scale earthquake that occurred January 12, 2010, on the West Indian island of Hispaniola, comprising the countries of Haiti and the Dominican Republic. Most severely affected was Haiti, occupying the western third of the island. An exact death toll proved elusive in the ensuing chaos.

What kind of buildings were destroyed in the Haiti earthquake?
--------------------------------------------------------------

Most of Port-au-Prince’s municipal government buildings were destroyed or heavily damaged in the earthquake, including the City Hall, which was described by the Washington Post as, “a skeletal hulk of concrete and stucco, sagging grotesquely to the left.”.
