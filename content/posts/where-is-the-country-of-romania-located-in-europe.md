Where is the country of Romania located in Europe?
--------------------------------------------------

Geography of Romania. Located in Southeastern Europe, bordering on the Black Sea, the country is halfway between the equator and the North Pole and equidistant from the westernmost part of Europe—the Atlantic Coast —and the most easterly—the Ural Mountains. Romania has 3,195 kilometers of border.

How big of an area does Romania have?
-------------------------------------

Romania covers an area of 92,046 square mile and is located in Europe. Romania sits at the crossroads of various European regions which has greatly influenced the culture of the citizens. Romania’s territory encompasses several islands such as the Great Brăila Island that occupies an area of about 274 square miles.

Where are the mountains and plains in Romania?
----------------------------------------------

As can be observed on the physical map of Romania, the country’s landscape is almost evenly divided among mountains, hills, and plains. As for mountains, the medium-sized Carpathians extend through the center of the country in a wide arc, and they are the source of several rivers.

How long is the border between Bulgaria and Romania?
----------------------------------------------------

Bulgaria-Romania Border. Romania’s border with Bulgaria is located on the southern side of the country. The Romania–Bulgaria border is 392 miles long, and part of the border is marked by Europe’s second longest river, the Danube.
