Where did the city of Rochester MN get its name?
------------------------------------------------

The area developed as a stagecoach stop between Saint Paul, Minnesota, and Dubuque, Iowa near the Zumbro River. The community was founded by George Head and his wife Henrietta who built log cabin Head’s Tavern in 1854 and named the city after his hometown of Rochester, New York.
