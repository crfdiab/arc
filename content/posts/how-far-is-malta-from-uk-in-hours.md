How far is Malta from UK in hours?
----------------------------------

On average, direct flights from the UK to Malta take between 3 hours 10 minutes and 3 hours 30 minutes. It can be cheaper to fly to Malta indirect; flights with stops take from 4 hours 35 minutes to 9 hours or more.
