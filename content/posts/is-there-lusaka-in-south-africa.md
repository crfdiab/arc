Is there Lusaka in South Africa?
--------------------------------

Lusaka (/luːˈsɑːkə/ loo-SAH-kə) is the capital and largest city of Zambia. It is one of the fastest developing cities in southern Africa. Lusaka is in the southern part of the central plateau at an elevation of about 1,279 metres (4,196 ft)….Lusaka.

How much does it cost to travel from Zambia to South Africa?
------------------------------------------------------------

How much is bus from Lusaka to South Africa?
--------------------------------------------

Shalom Bus Services operates a bus from Lusaka to Johannesburg once daily. Tickets cost R 755 and the journey takes 20h 27m. Juldan Motors also services this route once daily.

Does a South African need a visa to visit Zambia?
-------------------------------------------------

All visitors to Zambia need to be in possession of a passport valid for at least six months from their date of departure. Citizens of South Africa and Zimbabwe can obtain Zambian visas upon arrival for free; for all other nationalities, tourist visas are available at all major borders, airports and sea ports.
