Which mountain range divides the Antarctica into two parts?
-----------------------------------------------------------

Trans-Antarctic Mountains  
The Trans-Antarctic Mountains divide Antarctica into two parts, East Antarctica and West Antarctica.

Which is the third major mountain range in Antarctica?
------------------------------------------------------

The third major range is called the Transantarctic Mountains, which runs north to south and splits the continent into East and West Antarctica. The Transantarctic Mountains run the full length of western Antarctica, dividing the continent into East and West.
