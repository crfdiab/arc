Why is Ireland called Eire?
---------------------------

What was the result of the Irish War of Independence?
-----------------------------------------------------

The Irish War of Independence led to the Anglo-Irish Treaty, between the British government and representatives of the Irish Republic. It was signed on 6 December 1921.
