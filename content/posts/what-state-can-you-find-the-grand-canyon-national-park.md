What state can you find the Grand Canyon National Park?
-------------------------------------------------------

Arizona  
Grand Canyon is in the northwest corner of Arizona, close to the borders of Utah and Nevada. The Colorado River, which flows through the canyon, drains water from seven states, but the feature we know as Grand Canyon is entirely in Arizona.

What is the closest city to Grand Canyon National Park?
-------------------------------------------------------

Flagstaff  
Flagstaff is the closest major city near the Grand Canyon, located about 80 miles from both the south and east entrance stations to the South Rim.
