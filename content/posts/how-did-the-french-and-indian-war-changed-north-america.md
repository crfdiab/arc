How did the French and Indian War changed North America?
--------------------------------------------------------

The French and Indian War began in 1754 and ended with the Treaty of Paris in 1763. The war provided Great Britain enormous territorial gains in North America, but disputes over subsequent frontier policy and paying the war’s expenses led to colonial discontent, and ultimately to the American Revolution.

--------------------------------------------------

How did the American Revolution change the relationship between Britain and its colonies?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This war changed the relationship between Britain and its American colonies by tightening its grip on the colonies politically, imposing different taxis on the colonies economically, and thus changing the colonists ideology about the british government.
