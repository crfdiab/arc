Does the Pennsylvania National Guard pay for college?
-----------------------------------------------------

EAP is state funded program; it provides educational assistance for eligible members of the Pennsylvania National Guard. The EAP award amount can be applied to tuition costs, fees, books and room and board, up to the Allowable Cost of Attendance for members using FTA and GI Bill (Chapter 33) for the same term.

Is the Army National Guard better than the Air Guard?
-----------------------------------------------------
