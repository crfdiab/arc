Where does the Amazon River begin and end?
------------------------------------------

The Amazon River begins with streams in the Andes Mountains in Peru and flows into the Atlantic Ocean about 4,000 miles (6,437 km) away. The Amazon River and its watershed encompass an area of 2,720,000 square miles (7,050,000 sq km).
