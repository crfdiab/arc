Does Congo have good healthcare?
--------------------------------

The country has 0.28 physicians, and 1.91 nurses and midwives per 10,000 population. The health sector workforce is also aging and the quality of work has diminished (Direction Générale de la Santé, 2009). The northern rural areas of the country have the fewest health workers, particularly physicians (1.1-2.6%).
