How can I know my SSS ID number?
--------------------------------

Simply login to your SSS account on the website or mobile app and you will immediately see your SSS number. On the My. SSS website, your SSS number is located at the upper-left portion of the page, just below your name. On the SSS mobile app, the SSS number can be found on the upper-right corner of the screen.

How can I login to SSS?
-----------------------

Step 1: Go to sss.gov.ph, then proceed to member log in. Type in your registered user ID and password in the Member Login portal, then submit. Be careful when entering your password as it is case-sensitive. For smooth, optimal viewing and functionality, the Social Security System recommends using Internet Explorer.

Is SSS number and SSS ID number the same?
-----------------------------------------

No, they’re different. The Common Reference Number (CRN) is issued to SSS members who apply for a Unified Multi-Purpose ID (UMID). It links the ID numbers of SSS, GSIS, Pag-IBIG Fund, and PhilHealth. Meanwhile, the SSS number has 10 digits in this format: ##-#######-#.
