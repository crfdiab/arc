Is Afghanistan a developed or developing country?
-------------------------------------------------

Despite holding over $1 trillion in proven untapped mineral deposits, Afghanistan remains one of the least developed countries in the world. Its unemployment rate is over 23% and about half of its population lives below the poverty line.

What impact has conflict had on Afghanistan’s development?
----------------------------------------------------------

The war has exacerbated the effects of poverty, malnutrition, poor sanitation, lack of access to health care, and environmental degradation on Afghans’ health.
