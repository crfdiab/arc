How do I get from Tel Aviv airport to hotel?
--------------------------------------------

The fastest and most reliable way to get from Tel Aviv airport to the city centre of Tel Aviv is by taking a Ben Gurion airport taxi. The journey time will be around 20 minutes and you will be taken directly to the door of your hotel with no stress of hassle.

How much is a taxi to Ben Gurion airport?
-----------------------------------------

Taxis run 24-7 from the airport to Tel Aviv and cost between 110-190 shekels ($26-$50) depending on the day and time and the number of passengers and suitcases.

How do I get from Tel Aviv airport to Jerusalem?
------------------------------------------------

Getting from Tel Aviv Ben Gurion Airport to Jerusalem. There are three main ways to travel from Ben Gurion Airport to Jerusalem. Your first option is by shuttle bus, which costs €4 and takes approximately 1 hour to reach downtown Jerusalem. Your second option is in a Tel Aviv airport taxi.
