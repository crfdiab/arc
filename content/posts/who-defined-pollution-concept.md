Who Defined pollution concept?
------------------------------

Yapp (1972) defined pollution as ‘the presence of matter or energy in an unusual or unintended place’. Frequently pollution is caused by the acceleration of a natural phenomenon or by the increase in concentration of naturally occurring substances as a result of man’s activities.

What is the root word of pollution?
-----------------------------------

Pollute comes from the Latin word pollut-, meaning “soiled.” When you pollute something, it’s soiled or contaminated, often by something poisonous. You might have heard the word pollution — pollution is the stuff that pollutes the environment.
