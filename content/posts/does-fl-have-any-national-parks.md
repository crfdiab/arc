Where is the Everglades National Park in Florida?
-------------------------------------------------

Reference no. 374. Everglades National Park is an American national park that protects the southern twenty percent of the original Everglades in Florida. The park is the largest tropical wilderness in the United States, and the largest wilderness of any kind east of the Mississippi River.
