How does Portia persuade Brutus?
--------------------------------

Portia proves her strength to Brutus by showing him that she has the ability to conceal such a painful wound. She then asks her husband, “Can I bear that with patience, and not my husband’s secrets?” (2.1. 310-311).

What is Portia’s purpose in her conversation with Brutus in Act II Scene I?
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Portia is worried about why Brutus has been in such a nasty mood. She wants to know what is bothering him. Brutus initially tries to get Portia to leave him alone. Portia convinces Brutus that he should reveal to her the matter because she is his wife and they must trust each other.

What does Portia do to get Brutus to confide in her?
----------------------------------------------------

Portia knows he is lying and is upset that Brutus won’t confide in her. She calls on her position as his wife to convince him to confide in her. She insists that marriage is more that just sharing meals and a bed. It is about sharing confidences.
