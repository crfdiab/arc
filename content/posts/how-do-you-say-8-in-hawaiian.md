What are the colors in Hawaiian?
--------------------------------

Colors keiki (children’s) tee identifies the colors as taught in the Hawaiian color song:

- Ula’ula – Red.
- Melemele – Yellow.
- Poni – Purple.
- Polu – Blue.
- ‘Ele’ele – Black.
- ‘Akala – Pink.
- ‘Alani – Orange.
- Ke’oke’o – White.
