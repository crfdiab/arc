How long does it take for a letter to get from Florida to California?
---------------------------------------------------------------------

2 to 3 days is a normal amount of time for a regular piece of mail with a stamp on it. A minimum of three days for first class letter mail, a little longer for standard mail. Media mail usually a week to ten days or longer.

How long does it take to get a letter from New York to San Francisco?
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Generally speaking, most domestic letter mail (including postcards) can take from 1 to 5 days depending on the destination (and distance from the sender).

How long does it take for mail to be delivered between San Francisco?
---------------------------------------------------------------------

A lot depends on “the last mile”, meaning the actual delivery person. Mail service between mail hubs (San Francisco and San Diego each have hubs where all mail is sorted and handled by machines and transported in bulk) is fast and efficient (think 1.5-hour plane flights) but slows down to accommodate the hand delivery at the receiving end.

-------------------------------------------------------

How long does it take for Priority Mail to arrive?

How long does it take for mail to get to my ZIP code?
-----------------------------------------------------
