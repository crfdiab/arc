How is ground water an example of a renewable resource?
-------------------------------------------------------

Ground water is an example of renewable resource because it is replenished by nature, for example by rain.However, if we use more than what is being replenished by rain then we would be overusing the resource and this may lead to the exhaustion of groundwater after a period of time and thereby becoming a non-renewable resource.
