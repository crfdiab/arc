How much is a taxi from Abu Dhabi to Sharjah airport?
-----------------------------------------------------

The quickest way to get from Abu Dhabi to Sharjah Airport (SHJ) is to taxi which costs AED 320 – AED 390 and takes 1h 36m.

-----------------------------------------------
