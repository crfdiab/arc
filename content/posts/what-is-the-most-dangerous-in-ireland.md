Is there a housing crisis in Ireland now?
-----------------------------------------

The housing crisis in Ireland is a hot topic. Whilst, post-recession, property building has finally resumed, there remains an insanely low number of affordable housing options. Dublin remains the worst offender for this – an issue only aggravated by the excessive cost of living, but the problem exists country-wide.
