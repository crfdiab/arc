Which is the largest island in the Mediterranean Sea?

Is the island of Sardinia part of Italy?
----------------------------------------

Sardinia is located west of the Italian Peninsula and south of the French island of Corsica. Like Sicily, Sardinia is also a region of Italy and has some degree of domestic autonomy.
