Can Lebanese travel to Mexico?
------------------------------

Lebanese citizens, like many other nationalities, must obtain a visa in advance if they plan to spend their vacation in Mexico. The document is available only at the nearest Mexican diplomatic mission. At the same time, a Mexico tourist card is required as well.

Who needs a passport for Mexico?
--------------------------------

All foreigners, regardless their nationality, are required to present a valid and not expired passport or travel document when entering Mexico (traveling by air, land or sea). The Mexican government only requires that your passport must be valid during the entirety of your trip.

Can I travel without a passport to Mexico?
------------------------------------------

The Mexican government may not let U.S. citizens who are driving or walking to Mexico enter the country at land border crossings if they do not have a U.S. passport book or U.S. passport card.

Is it easy to get a visa for Mexico?
------------------------------------

Mexico visa application is fairly simple. It costs $44 USD and takes about two business days for processing. Mexico tourist visa is valid for 180-days, multiple-entry.
