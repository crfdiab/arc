What do people do in the tropical rainforest?
---------------------------------------------

While they still depend on the forest for traditional hunting and gathering, most Amerindians, as American indigenous people are called, grow crops (like bananas, manioc, and rice), use western goods (like metal pots, pans, and utensils), and make regular trips to towns and cities to bring foods and wares to market.

How can humans use the rainforest in a sustainable way?
-------------------------------------------------------

What do people wear in the rain forest?
---------------------------------------

Tribes with little contact with outsiders do not wear clothing at all. By contrast, westerners who visit the rain forest generally adorn themselves in the modern, moisture-wicking, synthetic fibers to cope with the heat and humidity.
