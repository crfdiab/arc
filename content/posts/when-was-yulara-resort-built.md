When was Yulara resort built?
-----------------------------

1982 and 1984  
The first stage of the resort was built between 1982 and 1984 for the Northern Territory Government by Yulara Development Company Ltd., at a cost of A$130 million. The resort was designed by Philip Cox &amp; Associates and won the Royal Australian Institute of Architects (RAIA) Sir Zelman Cowen Award in 1984.
