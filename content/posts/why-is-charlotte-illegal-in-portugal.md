Why is the name Chloe banned in Iceland?
----------------------------------------

Icelandic parents have some seriously strict requirements when it comes to baby names. Names must fit with Icelandic grammar, so any name with a C, Q or W in it is banned. This means there are no babies called Bruce, Charlotte, Chris, Chloe, William or Jack.
