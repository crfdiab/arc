Is Antarctica the southernmost?
-------------------------------

Often described as a continent of superlatives, Antarctica is not only the world’s southernmost continent. It is also the world’s highest, driest, windiest, coldest, and iciest continent.

-----------------------------

Which is the southernmost point in the world?
---------------------------------------------

Tierra del Fuego- The Southernmost Point in the World Isla Grande de Tierra del Fuego, or simply Tierra del Fuego, is a territory where it is hard to distinguish myth from reality, pure legend from objective truth. It is possibly this uncertainty, this almost magical atmosphere of mystery that covers the island, that draws so many tourists.

Which is the southernmost country outside of Antarctica?
--------------------------------------------------------

The former Argentine base Corbeta Uruguay (59°28′ S) in South Sandwich Islands was the southernmost settlement outside Antarctica from 1976–1982. There are many research stations in Antarctica, both permanent and summer only.

Which is the southernmost point of the country of Argentina?
------------------------------------------------------------

The undisputed southernmost point of Argentina is Islote Blanco (55°3′51″S 66°33′06″W). It lies on an area of 1 hectare and is located on the Province of Tierra del Fuego, in the department of Ushuaia.
