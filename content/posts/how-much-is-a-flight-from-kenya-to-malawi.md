Do I need a visa to Malawi from Kenya?
--------------------------------------

Citizens of Kenya do not require a visa to visit Malawi for a stay of up to 90 days. Prospective visitors must have a passport valid for at least 6 months beyond their stay in Malawi. On entry into Malawi, visitors with foreign currency will be asked to declare it.
