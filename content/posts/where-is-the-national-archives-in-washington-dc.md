Where is the National Archives in Washington DC?
------------------------------------------------

Open daily 10 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Located at 701 Constitution Ave., NW, Washington, DC 20408. Admission is always free. The museum wing of the National Archives, the National Archives Museum is the home of the Declaration of Independence, Constitution, and Bill of Rights.

When is the National Archives Research room open?
-------------------------------------------------

Research Room: Monday – Friday, 8:45 a.m. to 5:45 p.m. Please see the National Archives Museum website for the latest information. Find links to finding aids, military records, online research tools, ordering information, and much more.

When did the National Archives start digitizing documents?
----------------------------------------------------------

On January 10, 2007, the National Archives and Fold3.com (formerly Footnote) launched a pilot project to digitize historic documents from the National Archives holdings.
