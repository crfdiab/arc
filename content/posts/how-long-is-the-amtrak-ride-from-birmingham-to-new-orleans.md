How long is the Amtrak ride from Birmingham to New Orleans?
-----------------------------------------------------------

around 7 hours and 59 minutes  
Trip Summary Traveling by train from Birmingham to New Orleans usually takes around 7 hours and 59 minutes, but some trains might arrive slightly earlier or later than scheduled.

How do I get from Birmingham to New Orleans?
--------------------------------------------

Amtrak operates a train from Birmingham to New Orleans Union Passenger Terminal once daily. Tickets cost $45 – $70 and the journey takes 7h 59m. Alternatively, you can take a bus from Birmingham to New Orleans via Montgomery in around 8h 15m.

Do trains go faster at night?
-----------------------------

Freight trains will sometimes go faster during the nighttime, the reason is that during the night, there are less passenger trains on the tracks. While there are not so many passenger trains traveling during the nighttime, freight trains may run with fewer stops.
