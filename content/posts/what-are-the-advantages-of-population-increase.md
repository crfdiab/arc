What are the advantages and disadvantages of population growth?
---------------------------------------------------------------

The advantages of population growth include increased economic development, availability of labor and possibility of innovation. However, population growth may result in high unemployment rates, environmental degradation, food shortage and high dependency rates. Advantages of Population Growth.
