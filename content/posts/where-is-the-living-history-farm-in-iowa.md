Where is the living history farm in Iowa?
-----------------------------------------

Living History Farms is a 500-acre open-air museum located in Urbandale, Iowa, United States.

What does the Living History Museum in Iowa do?
-----------------------------------------------

The museum’s mission is to educate visitors and demonstrate the past 300 years of Iowa’s agricultural history. As its name implies, the museum follows the methodology of living history in depicting the lives of people living on farms in the years of 1700, 1850, and 1900, engaging in various agricultural activities.

--------------------------------------------------
