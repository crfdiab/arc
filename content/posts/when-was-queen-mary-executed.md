When was Queen Mary executed?
-----------------------------

February 8, 1587  
Mary, Queen of Scots/Date of death  
Mary was finally executed at Fotheringhay Castle in Northamptonshire on 8 February 1587, at the age of 44. She was buried in Peterborough Cathedral, but in 1612 her son James VI and I had her body exhumed and placed in the vault of King Henry VII’s Chapel in Westminster Abbey.

What Queen did Elizabeth execute in 1586?
-----------------------------------------

Mary, Queen of Scots  
On 24 November 1586 a delegation from the Lords and Commons went to meet Queen Elizabeth I at Richmond Palace, their mission: to convince her to order the execution of Mary, Queen of Scots. The Parliament of 1586 was called specifically because of Mary’s complicity in the Babington Plot in August that year.
