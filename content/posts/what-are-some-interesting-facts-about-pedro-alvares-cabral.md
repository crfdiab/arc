What are some interesting facts about Pedro Alvares Cabral?
-----------------------------------------------------------

What Are Some Interesting Facts About Pedro Alvares Cabral? Pedro Alvares Cabral was an explorer most famous as the first European to see Brazil. Cabral saw the coast of Brazil on April 22, 1500, and claimed the land for Portugal. He explored the coast for 12 days, landing just north of what is now Rio de Janeiro.
