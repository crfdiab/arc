What does the Isthmus of Panama connect when was the Panama Canal built and why?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Panama Canal (Spanish: Canal de Panamá) is an artificial 82 km (51 mi) waterway in Panama that connects the Atlantic Ocean with the Pacific Ocean. The canal cuts across the Isthmus of Panama and is a conduit for maritime trade. It is now managed and operated by the government-owned Panama Canal Authority.
