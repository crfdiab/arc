What were the three main US regions in the early 1800s?
-------------------------------------------------------

Three main regions developed in the United States– North, South, and the West.

What were the early 1800s like in America?
------------------------------------------

The early 1800s saw the United States quickly grow in size. New immigrants and new land meant a bigger and stronger country. It also meant displacing thousands of Native Americans and the continued spread of slavery.

Why did people come to the United States in the late 1800s?
-----------------------------------------------------------

Immigration to the United States, 1851-1900. In the late 1800s, people in many parts of the world decided to leave their homes and immigrate to the United States. Fleeing crop failure, land and job shortages, rising taxes, and famine, many came to the U. S. because it was perceived as the land of economic opportunity.
