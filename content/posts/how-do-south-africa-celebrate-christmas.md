How do South Africa celebrate Christmas?
----------------------------------------

Christmas Day in South Africa Some people start the morning with a church service, while others get straight to opening their Christmas presents. After the morning of activities, friends and families gather together for a Christmas lunch or dinner, followed by some backyard games like cricket, or a well-earned nap!

What is Christmas known as in South Africa?
-------------------------------------------

Merry Christmas in South African (Afrikaans) “Geseende Kersfees,” and Santa Claus is known as “Sinterklaas” or “Kersvader.”

Who celebrates Christmas in South Africa?
-----------------------------------------

Christmas Day, celebrated on December 25 in Catholic, Protestant, and most Orthodox churches, is a public holiday in South Africa. On this day Christians commemorate the birth of Jesus Christ in Bethlehem. The date is traditional and is not considered to be the actual date of his birth.

When do people celebrate Christmas in South Africa?
---------------------------------------------------

Christmas celebrations in South Africa start off with presents on Christmas morning, which is on the 25th December, followed by a traditional Christmas dinner or braai in the afternoon. South Africans usually have dinner on Christmas Day (25th December) rather than on Christmas Eve (24th December).

How is Christmas celebrated in Africa according to the Coptic calendar?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

On Christmas Day carols are sung from Ghana to South Africa. Meats are roasted, gifts are exchanged, and people travel far and wide to visit family. However, in many countries the celebration is very different than it is in North America or Europe. Coptic Christians in Ethiopia and Egypt celebrate Christmas according to the Coptic calendar.

What foods are eaten at Christmas in South Africa?
--------------------------------------------------

The Christmas meal is either turkey, duck, roast beef, mince pies or suckling pig with yellow rice &amp; raisins and vegetables, followed by Christmas Pudding or a traditional South African desert called Malva Pudding (sometimes also called Lekker Pudding) – get the recipe.

When do Christmas flowers bloom in South Africa?
------------------------------------------------

Because South Africa is in the Southern Hemisphere, Christmas comes in the summer. So there’s lots of sun and beautiful flowers in full bloom.
