Can you travel with green card without visa?
--------------------------------------------

Citizens of the US are allowed to fly to 184 countries worldwide without the need of having a visa. However, US citizens who have a green card, are allowed to travel without the need for a visa to some non-US countries.

Can I go to Korea with green card?
----------------------------------

U.S. citizens can visit the Republic of Korea without a visa for duration of up to 90 days or less if the purpose of the trip is for tourism, business meeting, attendance at a conference or visiting families or relatives. \*US Green Card Holders are not applicable to the Korea-US Visa Waiver Program.
