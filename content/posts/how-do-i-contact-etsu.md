What is ETSU official form of communication with all students?
--------------------------------------------------------------

ETSU provides each student, faculty, and staff member with an official university-assigned email account. All official university communications will be sent to the university email address. Faculty, staff, and students may assume that official ETSU email is a valid communication mechanism.

How was the Gray Fossil Site Discovered?
----------------------------------------

Like many fossil localities, the Gray Fossil Site was discovered by accident. In the year 2000, a Tennessee Department of Transportation (TDOT) construction crew exposed the black clay of the site during a road reconstruction project.

What fossils have been found in Tennessee?
------------------------------------------

Like many dinosaur-poor states near the east coast, Tennessee is unusually rich in the fossils of much less impressive animals—the crinoids, brachiopods, trilobites, corals and other small marine creatures that populated the shallow seas and lakes of North America over 300 million years ago, during the Devonian.
