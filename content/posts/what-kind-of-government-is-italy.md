How are branches of a company taxed in Italy?
---------------------------------------------

As opposed to the establishment of an Italian company, a branch is not a separate legal entity but a foreign “unit” of the mother company. For tax purposes, branches are considered as permanent establishments and are therefore subject to taxation in Italy.
