Where is Andorra located in the world?
--------------------------------------

Andorra is a sovereign landlocked southwest European microstate. It is geographically positioned both in the Northern and Eastern hemispheres of the Earth. Andorra is situated on the Iberian Peninsula among the eastern slopes of the Pyrenees mountain range.

What kind of weather does Andorra have in winter?
-------------------------------------------------

During winter precipitation occurs in the form of snow beginning in November and ending in Late March or early April. The winter snow is vital for the tourism industry with the country providing some of the best skiing in the Pyrenees. During the summer the Azores anticyclone is experienced, and thunderstorms may occur in the afternoons.
