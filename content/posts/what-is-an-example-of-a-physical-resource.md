What is a physical resource in economics?
-----------------------------------------

Physical resources are tangible items that are used in the operation of the business. Some of the resources are used to operate the business or provide products and services. Some of the most common physical resources include raw materials, buildings and facilities, machinery, energy, and supplies.

What are physical resources in social studies?
----------------------------------------------

Physical resources refer to the things in nature such as land, water, plants, minerals, animals, wind and sunlight. These resources are harnessed for the production of goods. Land is used for agriculture. Water is used for domestic and commercial purposes.
