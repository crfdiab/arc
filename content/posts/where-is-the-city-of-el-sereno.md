What district is El Sereno?
---------------------------

Council District 14  
The El Sereno area of Council District 14 covers the 90032 ZIP Code, including the neighborhoods of Rose Hills, University Hills, and Hillside Village; as well as the adjacent neighborhoods of Hermon and Monterey Hills.
