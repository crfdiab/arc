What were mine owners called in South Africa?

When did white miners go on strike in South Africa?
---------------------------------------------------

In early 1922, white South African workers in the Witwatesrand gold mining region went on strike. The strike soon became a violent rebellion—sometimes known as the Rand Revolt—that pitted the white miners against the mine owners and the government.
