How can I convert my CGPA into percentage?
------------------------------------------

To convert CGPA to percentage, all you need to do is multiply your CGPA by 9.5. This formula is provided on the official website of CBSE for class IX and X. For example, if you have got 9.4 CGPA then the equivalent percentage would be 9.4\*9.5 = 89.3%.

How do you convert CGPA to percentage in college?
-------------------------------------------------

The formula to convert CGPA to percentage is: Percentage = 7.25 \* CGPA +11. For example, a student earned an 8.5 CGPA in his exam. To find the Percentage, use the given formulae: 7.25\*8.5+11. The Percentage we get is 72.6.
