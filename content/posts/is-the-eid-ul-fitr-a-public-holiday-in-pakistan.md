------------------------------------------

How is the festival of Eid al-Fitr celebrated?
----------------------------------------------

The festival of Eid al-Fitr is celebrated by first attending the mosque in the morning for Eid prayer. This is followed by visiting relatives and neighbours. Children receive presents and money from elder members of the family, relatives and neighbours.
