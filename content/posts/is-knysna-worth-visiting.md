Is Knysna worth visiting?
-------------------------

There are loads of fun things to do in Knysna – either as a holiday in itself, or as part of a longer trip along the famous Garden Route (definitely want to drive that one day). Here are a few reasons why Knysna is definitely worth visiting if you’re planning a trip to South Africa.

Does Knysna have an airport?
----------------------------

The nearest airport to Knysna is in George, which is 60km away. Located just 10km from George’s town centre, the airport only serves domestic flights in South Africa. For international flights to/from George Airport, flights will need to be booked via Johannesburg or Cape Town.

Which airport is closer to Knysna?
----------------------------------

George Airport  
About George South Africa George Airport is also the closest airport to the popular towns of Knysna, Groot Brak and Oudtshoorn.

How long is the drive to George from Cape Town?
-----------------------------------------------

Yes, the driving distance between Cape Town to George is 432 km. It takes approximately 4h 32m to drive from Cape Town to George.

Does Knysna have a beach?
-------------------------

Situated along one of the world’s most beautiful coastlines, Knysna is in the heart of the famous Garden Route, which is home to an array of ruggedly beautiful shores and pristine beaches.

Can you drink the water in Knysna?
----------------------------------

The Knysna Municipality says its water supply is safe for human consumption. This comes after there was an attempt to sabotage the municipality’s water supply.

Is Knysna in George?
--------------------

It is near the towns of Plettenberg Bay and George, where there are airports. Knysna is also home to Mitchell’s Brewery although the company no longer brews beers in the town.

How do you get to Knysna?
-------------------------

The fastest way to reach Knysna is by boarding a flight to the nearest airport in the city. Knysna enjoys excellent connectivity to domestic hubs. However, tourists entering in from international quarters need to first get to Johannesburg and then book a flight to the local airport at George town.

How many kilos is it from Cape Town to George?
----------------------------------------------

Distance from Cape Town to George. The driving distance between Cape Town and George is 429.5km and it would take you 5 Hours 22 minutes if you average 80 km/h (49 mph).

Is Plettenberg Bay Safe?
------------------------

Plettenberg Bay and the Cape Garden Route especially can be safely visited by tourists provided they take basic common-sense precautions (for example not walking alone in deserted areas at night and being circumspect about how much photographic equipment or flashy jeweler you carry.
