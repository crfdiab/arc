How to check Nalanda Open University Result?
--------------------------------------------

Procedure to Check Nalanda Open University Result Candidates have to click on UG &amp; PG Result after displaying the result portal and then select the appeared course for the result. The result will display to the candidates after submitting the details. Applicants can take the print of their results for future use.

How can I get admission in Nalanda Open University?
---------------------------------------------------

The admission form (Annexure-A) and prospectus may be obtained through Demand Draft of Rs. 450.00 from the sales counters of the University or by sending the demand draft of Rs. 400.00 by registered post, drawn in favour of Nalanda Open University, Payable at Patna. No money order or postal order is accepted.

--------------------------------------

Who built Nalanda?
------------------

Nalanda Mahavihara was founded by Kumargupta I of the Gupta dynasty in 5th century CE. It was patronized by various rulers including King Harshavardhana of Kannauj (7th century CE) and the Pala rulers (8th – 12th century CE) as well as various scholars.
