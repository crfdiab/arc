How do I call a Spanish mobile from a UK mobile?
------------------------------------------------

How to Call Spain from the UK

1. Dial the international call prefix. For calls from the UK this is 00 (or ‘+’ from mobile phones).
2. Dial the country code for Spain – 34.
3. Dial the number of the person/business, omitting the first zero if there is one.

How to call a UK mobile phone overseas?
---------------------------------------

To call a UK mobile phone that is currently overseas, just call its full phone number as normal. Do not try to add any additional prefixes or international calling codes. No matter where in the world a UK mobile phone is taken to, it will keep the same UK telephone number.
