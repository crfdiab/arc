What is the cheapest way to ship a small package in the US?
-----------------------------------------------------------

Cheapest shipping option for small packages: U.S. Postal Service’s Priority Mail. Best shipping option for medium to large packages: The Postal Service’s Priority Mail. Best shipping option for very large packages: FedEx Ground or UPS Ground. Cheapest option for overnight shipping: Postal Service Priority Mail Express.

What is the cheapest way to send a small package?
-------------------------------------------------

First Class Mail envelopes, flat rate envelopes, and USPS Priority Mail offer the lowest rates for small packages. Another option is to use cubic pricing. Cubic pricing is a special USPS service that offers lower shipping rates when sending small, heavy items that are under 20lbs with USPS Priority Mail.
