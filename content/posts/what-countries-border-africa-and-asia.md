Are there any countries outside Africa that border Africa?
----------------------------------------------------------

None. Egypt is the only African country with a land border outside Africa, but the border between Africa and Asia runs through Egypt, and Egypt cannot border itself. Which countries border Africa? None.

Which is the only country that does not have a border with another country?
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

China shares an international border with 16 neighbors, more than any other country in the world. Nearly landlocked in the center of Africa, the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) borders ten countries over 6,667 miles. Ecuador and Chile are the only countries on the South American continent that don’t share a border with Brazil.

Where are the borders of the Asian continent?
---------------------------------------------

This article will explore the complex subject of Asian borders, and, hopefully, help you understand the geographical limits of that huge continent. Asia is bordered by three oceans, the Indian Ocean to the south, the Pacific Ocean to the east, and the Arctic Ocean to the north. It is Asia’s overland border that’s the tricky part.
