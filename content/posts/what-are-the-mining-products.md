What became the most important mining resource in Arkansas?
-----------------------------------------------------------

Bauxite–an aluminum ore–is an important natural resource of Arkansas and was named as its official mineral in 1967. According to Thomas Leonard Watson in the book “A Preliminary Report on the Bauxite Deposits of Arkansas,” the bauxite deposits of Arkansas were first discovered by the Geological Survey in 1891.

What is done with the waste products from mining?
-------------------------------------------------

The gangue and other rock waste are disposed of by storing them in waste piles or in the base of tailings dam embankments. The major portion of the waste rock is disposed of in piles at the source. The coarse coal refuse is removed from the preparation plant and disposed of in large piles or banks.
