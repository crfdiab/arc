Why is the band named Train?
----------------------------

Train has its origins in a chance meeting between singer Patrick Monahan and a Los Angeles band called the Apostles. The pair ultimately expanded their lineup to include Stafford as well as drummer Scott Underwood and bass player Charlie Colin; the new band dubbed itself Train and relocated to San Francisco.
