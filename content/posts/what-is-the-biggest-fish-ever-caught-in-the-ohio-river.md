Which is River forms the border between Indiana and Kentucky?
-------------------------------------------------------------

The United States contains thousands of rivers, many of which serve as important features of the physical geography of the country. Many state borders are defined in relation to rivers, and many individual states took their name after a nearby river of significance. The border between Indiana and Kentucky is formed by the Ohio River.

What is the border between Ohio and Kentucky?
---------------------------------------------

The Tug and Big Sandy rivers mark the boundary between Kentucky and West Virginia to the east and northeast. The Ohio River forms the boundary between the states of Illinois, Indiana, and Ohio to the north. The Mississippi River demarcates the short southwestern border with the state of Missouri.
