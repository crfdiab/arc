Why is there an ATM in Antarctica?
----------------------------------

Due to the frigid arctic weather, it’s impossible for service providers and cash vendors to fly cash in regularly. So, all cash is recycled by the McMurdo Station finance office. NCR sends the engineers from the United States because the customer, Wells Fargo, is a U.S. client. No ATM fees are charged.

What is the meaning of the word Antarctica?
-------------------------------------------

“Anti-” is commonly a synonym for the opposite in English as well as in Greek. Thus, Antarctica means opposite of Arctic land, “Anti-” plus the Arctic which forms a compound word.

Are there any countries that are part of Antarctica?
----------------------------------------------------

There are no countries in Antarctica, although seven nations claim different parts of it: New Zealand, Australia, France, Norway, the United Kingdom, Chile, and Argentina. The Antarctic also includes island territories within the Antarctic Convergence.

Which is the largest scientific station in Antarctica?
------------------------------------------------------

McMurdo is the largest station in Antarctica, capable of supporting 1,250 residents. Most of these residents are not scientists, but work to support station operations, construction, maintenance, and daily life. McMurdo has more than 80 buildings and operates like a small city.
