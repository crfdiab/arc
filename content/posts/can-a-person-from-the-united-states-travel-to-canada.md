Can a car be imported from the United States to Canada?
-------------------------------------------------------

For a vehicle to be imported into Canada from the United States, it must be on Transport Canada’s list of permissible makes and models. If you have any questions about your particular car model, contact Transport Canada about your questions.

Do you need a passport to travel to Canada?
-------------------------------------------

The Canadian government requires citizens of the United States to have a passport to fly to or transit through a Canadian airport, but not to enter Canada by land or by boat.
