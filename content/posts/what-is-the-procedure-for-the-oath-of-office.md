What is the process called when the President is sworn into office?
-------------------------------------------------------------------

In the Federal Government, in order for an official to take office, he or she must first take the oath of office; this is also known as a swearing-in ceremony. The official reciting the oath swears an allegiance to uphold the Constitution.

When do you take an oath of office?
-----------------------------------

To administer an oath to someone as part of the official process of taking public office. A noun or pronoun can be used between “swear” and “in.” The current president was sworn in after his predecessor died of a sudden heart attack.
