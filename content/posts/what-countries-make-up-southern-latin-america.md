What countries make up Southern Latin America?
----------------------------------------------

The continent generally includes twelve sovereign states: Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Guyana, Paraguay, Peru, Suriname, Uruguay, and Venezuela; two dependent territories: the Falkland Islands and South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands; and one internal territory: French Guiana.

Which is the largest country in South America?
----------------------------------------------

Brazil is the largest and most populous country in both South America and Latin America as a whole. It has a population of approximately 212 million, and a total land area of 8,358,140 sq. km. Brazil also has the biggest economy in both South America and Latin America.

Where are the countries of Latin America located?
-------------------------------------------------

Latin America is a vast region of the Western Hemisphere that stretches from the U.S.-Mexico border all the way down to the southern tip of South America. Thus, it includes Mexico, Central America, the Caribbean, and South America .
