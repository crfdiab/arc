Why is DC split into wards?
---------------------------

Hear this out loudPauseWhy is city divided into wards? This means that certain regions will have more number of citizens compared to another and thus, the representatives elected will not be of uniform representation. Thus, city is divided into wards based on population in order to ensure equal representation of voters.
