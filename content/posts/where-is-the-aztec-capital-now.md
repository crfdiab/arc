Where is the Aztec capital now?
-------------------------------

Mexico City  
Tenochtitlan was one of two Mexica āltepētl (city-states or polities) on the island, the other being Tlatelolco. The city is located in modern-day Mexico City.

What Aztec city was Mexico City built on top of?
------------------------------------------------

Tenochtitlan  
The Aztec built their capital city, Tenochtitlan, on Lake Texcoco.

Is Mexico City an Aztec?
------------------------

The city now known as Mexico City was founded as Mexico Tenochtitlan in 1325 and a century later became the dominant city-state of the Aztec Triple Alliance, formed in 1430 and composed of Tenochtitlan, Texcoco, and Tlacopan.

What evidence is there of the Aztecs in Mexico City?
----------------------------------------------------

Throughout downtown Mexico City, archaeologists have found some 40,000 artifacts, including mirrors made of shiny obsidian, Pacific turtle shells that were much-prized by the Aztecs, and precious jade-and-turquoise masks, all of which attest to the empire’s wealth.

Where are the Aztec ruins in Mexico City?
-----------------------------------------

Mexican archaeologists have found the remains of the homes of Aztec ruler Axayacatl and Spanish conquistador Hernan Cortes in Mexico city. The site is located under a historic building on the capital’s central plaza.

When did Tenochtitlan become the capital of Mexico?
---------------------------------------------------

The date 13 March 1325, was chosen in 1925 to celebrate the 600th anniversary of the city. The city was built on an island in what was then Lake Texcoco in the Valley of Mexico. The city was the capital of the expanding Aztec Empire in the 15th century until it was captured by the Spanish in 1521 .

Where is the Great Temple of the Aztecs?
----------------------------------------

Taylor McIntyre / © TripSavvy. Templo Mayor, the great temple of the Aztecs, stands in the heart of Mexico City. Many tourists miss out on visiting this outstanding archaeological site because they don’t realize it is there.

Which is the most visited city in Mexico?
-----------------------------------------

It is said that Templo Mayor was one of the central temples in the ancient Aztec capital of Tenochtitlan. Today, it is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Mexico.
