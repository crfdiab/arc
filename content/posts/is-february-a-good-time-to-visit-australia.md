Is February a good time to visit Australia?
-------------------------------------------

The best times to visit Sydney are September through November and from March to May. Thanks to Australia’s location in the Southern Hemisphere, Sydney’s warmest weather and peak tourist season falls between December and February (winter in the Northern Hemisphere).

How hot is Melbourne in February?
---------------------------------

around 78°F

What season is February in Australia?
-------------------------------------

In Australia, the seasons are defined by grouping the calendar months in the following way: Spring – the three transition months September, October and November. Summer – the three hottest months December, January and February. Autumn – the transition months March, April and May.
