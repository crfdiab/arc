Is Rune Factory 5 coming to us?
-------------------------------

Currently, Rune Factory 5 has been confirmed for release in North America during 2022, but the announcement during the E3 2021 Future Games Show did not include a firm month and date.

Is Rune Factory exclusive to Nintendo?
--------------------------------------

Is Rune Factory 5 delayed?
--------------------------

During the Future Games Show during E3 2021, XSeed revealed a new trailer for Rune Factory 5. Another trailer in September 2020 originally gave the West a 2021 release date. The initial delay in Japan came in early 2020, likely due to COVID.
