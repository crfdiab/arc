What are the main human features of Brazil?
-------------------------------------------

Human Characteristics of People from Brazil

- Their official language is Portuguese.

- In 2011 the literacy rate of Brazil was 88.6%.
- The Population of Brazil is 201,032,714 people.
- Brazil has the largest population in South America.

What are the physical features of the country of Brazil?
--------------------------------------------------------

What Are the Physical Features of Brazil? Brazil’s physical features are characterized by its highlands, plateaus and river basins. The region is not mountainous, but it is very diverse and typically has a humid tropical or subtropical climate.
