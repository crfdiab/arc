Which best describes the first organisms on Earth?
--------------------------------------------------

The first organisms on earth were simple prokaryotes. Prokaryotes are organisms which have no complex membrane bound organelles or nucleus. Modern bacteria and archaea are prokaryotic.

What are the characteristics of ecosystem?
------------------------------------------

Most ecosystems characteristically possess a well-defined soil, climate, flora and fauna (or communities) and have their own potential for adaptation, change and tolerance. The functioning of any ecosystem involves a series of cycles, e.g., the water cycle and the cycles of various nutrients.

What is the importance of ecosystems?
-------------------------------------
