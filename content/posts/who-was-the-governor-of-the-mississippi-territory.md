Who was the governor of the Mississippi Territory?
--------------------------------------------------

governor David Holmes  
With the last requirement for statehood completed, President James Monroe on December 10, 1817, signed the resolution that admitted Mississippi as the nation’s twentieth state. Territorial governor David Holmes won election as the state’s first governor.

Who was Mississippi’s first governor?
-------------------------------------

David Holmes  
In 1816, Congress decided to divide the territory into two states. The western half would become the state of Mississippi. The constitutional convention was held in Washington, Mississippi, in 1817. David Holmes served as president of the convention and was elected the first governor of the new state.
