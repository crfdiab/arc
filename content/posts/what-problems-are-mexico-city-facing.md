What problems are Mexico City facing?
-------------------------------------

Problematic housing, snarled traffic, and stubborn air pollution are three of the most prominent challenges in greater Mexico City, which is the largest metropolitan area in the Western Hemisphere and the fifth-largest in the world. They are the same problems that threaten to overwhelm megacities worldwide.

What are the biggest problems in Mexico City?
---------------------------------------------

Mexico: main issues according to experts 2019 During a survey carried out in June and July of 2019, more than half (55 percent) of the opinion leaders and prominent journalists surveyed in Mexico stated that insecurity, criminality and drug trafficking were the most important problems in the country.
