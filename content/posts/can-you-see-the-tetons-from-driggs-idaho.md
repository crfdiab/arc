Where is the Teton Valley?
--------------------------

Teton Valley is located on the west slope of the Teton Mountain Range in the western United States. Sometimes known as “The quiet side of the Tetons”, it is composed of the cities of Victor, Idaho, Driggs, Idaho, Tetonia, Idaho, and Alta, Wyoming.
