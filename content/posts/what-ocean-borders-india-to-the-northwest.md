Is Indian Ocean surrounded by North America?
--------------------------------------------

It is bound by northern coasts of North America and Eurasia. The Indian Ocean is the only ocean named after a country, that is, India. The shape of ocean is almost triangular. In the north, it is bound by Asia, in the west by Africa and in the east by Australia.

Where is most of the coastline of India located?
------------------------------------------------

Having a coastline of over 7,000 km (4,300 miles), most of India lies on a peninsula in southern Asia that protrudes into the Indian Ocean. India is bounded in the southwest by the Arabian Sea and in the southeast by the Bay of Bengal.
