Why is he called the Duke of York?

He was created Duke of York following the death of his elder brother, Prince Albert Victor, Duke of Clarence and Avondale. The title merged with the Crown when George succeeded his father as King George V. The title was created for the eighth time for Prince Andrew, second son of Queen Elizabeth II.
