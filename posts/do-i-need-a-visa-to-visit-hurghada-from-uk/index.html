Do I need a visa to visit Hurghada from UK?

Visas. British passport holders travelling to Egypt normally need a visa. You can get a visa before you travel from the official Visa2Egypt portal or your nearest Egyptian consulate. Tourist visas granted using the e-visa system are valid for a maximum of 3 months.

Can I apply for an Egyptian visa online?

To apply for a single or multiple-entry Egypt eVisa, applicants need to complete the online visa application form and pay a fee using a credit or debit card. Applicants should apply at least 7 days before their trip. Approved Egypt eVisas are sent to applicants by email once they have been processed.

Can you get an Egypt visa on arrival?

An Egypt visa is required for most travelers including American and British passport holders. Americans and citizens from 40 other countries can obtain an Egypt visa on arrival at the Cairo International airport at the bank kiosks before the immigration counters for $25 USD and are valid for visits up to 30 days.

Can I get Egypt visa on arrival?

The process of obtaining an Egypt visa on arrival for US citizens is the same as that for EU passport holders and nationals of other eligible countries. Once granted, the visa on arrival will allow international travelers to stay in Egypt for up to 30 days. The visa on arrival is also a single-entry visa.
