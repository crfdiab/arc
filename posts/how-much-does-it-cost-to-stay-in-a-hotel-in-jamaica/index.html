How much does it cost to stay in a hotel in Jamaica?

The average price for one person for accommodation in Jamaica is J$10,201. For two people sharing a typical double-occupancy hotel room, the average price for a hotel room in Jamaica is J$20,402.

What is the best part of Jamaica to stay in?

Montego Bay is often considered the best place to stay in Jamaica. It is the second largest city in Jamaica, and the airport has several direct flights from the United States. Montego Bay is also one of the main cruise ship ports in Jamaica.

Are hotels in Jamaica safe?

Safest Places to Visit in Jamaica. Jamaica is, against the popular belief, a safe country for tourist. Safe neighborhoods in Jamaica are pretty much all resorts and urban areas that are well developed. They don’t just give you great accommodation options, but usually also a 24/7 security guard on site.
