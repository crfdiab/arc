Why is PA National Guard being deployed?

Over 50 Pennsylvania National Guardsmen were placed on state active duty in March to provide subject matter expert planners to assist the Pennsylvania Emergency Management Agency and to provide transportation for 38 Pennsylvania residents who had been quarantined following a COVID-19 outbreak on a cruise ship.

Does the Pennsylvania National Guard pay for college?

EAP is state funded program; it provides educational assistance for eligible members of the Pennsylvania National Guard. The EAP award amount can be applied to tuition costs, fees, books and room and board, up to the Allowable Cost of Attendance for members using FTA and GI Bill (Chapter 33) for the same term.

Which state National Guard is the best?

Don’t mess with Texas’ National Guard. Texas has a number of capabilities that elevate the Lone Star State to the #1 position. Its sheer size is a significant factor — the Texas National Guard is host to nearly 21,000 troops, including its army and air components.

Is the Army National Guard better than the Air Guard?

The Air National Guard (ANG) tends to treat its people better and the level of discipline that is used and applied is slightly less than what you will find in the National Guard (NG). That is because the NG’s role and general mission revolves around ground combat and combat sustainment.
