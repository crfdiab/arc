What is the best way to get from EWR to JFK?

The best way to get from Newark Airport (EWR) to New York JFK Airport (JFK) without a car is to bus and subway via Sutphin Blvd-Archer Av-JFK Airport which takes 1h 8m and costs $22 – $30.

How can I transfer from EWR to JFK?

First, take the JFK AirTrain to Jamaica station, then, switch to the Long Island Railroad train and head to New York Penn Station. From here, you will switch to the NJ Transit train and travel to the Newark Airport Railway. Finally, take the Newark AirTrain from the Newark airport railroad to Terminal A, B or C.
