Where did Marco Polo go before he became an explorer?

Marco Polo. Marco Polo was a merchant in Venice before he became the famous explorer we know today. At 17, he left Venice with his father and uncle. They traveled the Silk Road to China where he met the great ruler Kublai Khan.

How long did Marco Polo stay in China?

Marco Polo spent 17 years in China before returning to Europe. Since one of the only historical pieces of evidence regarding his life and travels is his own writings, there is speculation that Marco Polo never actually made it to China.

What did Marco Polo do on the Silk Road?

Quick Facts: Marco Polo is known for traveling along the Silk Road to China, where he explored and documented much of Asia not yet explored by Europeans.

When was Marco Polo born and when did he die?

How long did it take Marco Polo to get to China?

Marco Polo traveled by land from Venice, Italy to China in 1271. It took him four years to get to his destination. He did not use a ship for the trip. Marco Polo spent 24 years traveling through Europe and Asia on this one trip.
