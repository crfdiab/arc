What causes the greatest amount of pollution?

The combustion of fossil fuels like coal, petroleum and other factory combustibles is a major cause of air pollution. Meanwhile, emissions caused by gasoline-burning vehicles – i.e. CO, CO², nitrogen oxides, particulates and water vapor – are also a significant source of air pollution.

Who are the top polluters of the oceans?

How is plastic pollution bad for the ocean?

Most plastic ocean pollution is floating between the water and beaches. Over time the plastic will break down into smaller pieces, hastened as the sun degrades the plastic fragments. As you might expect, ocean pollution is not a good thing for our marine wildlife (or humans).
