How long is the flight from London to Mexico City?

The total flight duration from London, United Kingdom to Mexico City, Mexico is 11 hours, 37 minutes. This assumes an average flight speed for a commercial airliner of 500 mph, which is equivalent to 805 km/h or 434 knots. It also adds an extra 30 minutes for take-off and landing.
