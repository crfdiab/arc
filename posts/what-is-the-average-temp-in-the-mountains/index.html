What is the average temp in the mountains?

In Mountain Ranch, the summers are hot, arid, and mostly clear and the winters are cold, wet, and partly cloudy. Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 37°F to 89°F and is rarely below 29°F or above 96°F.
