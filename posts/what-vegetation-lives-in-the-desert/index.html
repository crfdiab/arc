What vegetation lives in the desert?

Desert plants can be classified into three main categories: Cacti and Succulents, Wildflowers, and Trees, Shrubs, and Grasses.

What are characteristics of a desert plant?

Desert plants with thick leaves and stems are able to absorb water when it rains and continue growing even when rainfall is absent. Some desert plants have developed holding sacks in their stems that swell to hold water during those times when it is available.

What is the special quality of plants and animals that live in the desert Class 7?

What is the special quality of plants and animals that live in the desert ? The plants and animals living in the desert develop a special quality. They require less water than most others of their kind.

What kind of vegetation is found in the desert?

Cactus is the most common type of plant or shrub found in nearly all the deserts of the world. Desert biome is classified into 4 types, viz., Coastal desert, cold desert, semiarid desert, and hot and dry desert. The type of vegetation found differs according to the type of desert.

Are there any plants in the Sahara Desert?

However, there are still some areas in the Sahara desert that are lush, containing abundant trees and plants. A lot of greenery and abundant vegetation is found in the Nile valley. This is possible because this area receives adequate amount of water from the Nile river.

Why do plants grow in the hot desert?

Vegetation. Vegetation in hot deserts Firstly you would know that the hot desert’s soil is extremely poor and are limited to what the desert soil can grow. But usually plants that do grow would have really long roots to retrieve water better. They would also be able to store water in the stem and leaves.

What kind of plants grow in the Kalahari Desert?

Kalahari Desert Plants. In regards to trees, the most prevalent ones are acacia trees like the camelthorn tree. This tree grows almost exclusively in the Kalahari Desert and it is a symbol of the desert. Some cactus species like the Hoodia cactus is also present. Closer to the Okavango Delta there can be found baobabs as well.
