How big is the State University of New York?

The State University of New York, with 64 unique institutions, is the largest comprehensive system of higher education in the United States. How big is the New York State Education Building?

The 1870 allegorical mural, The Genius of America, was installed in Chancellors Hall of the New York State Education building in 1953. Measuring 29.5 feet-by-18 feet, the historical painting is a panorama of symbolic figures and objects that reflects sentiments and ideals about the United States at the turn of the 19 th Century.
