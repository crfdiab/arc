What is the physical geography of Maine?

As observed on the map, Maine is divided into three distinct areas: the Atlantic Ocean Coastal Lowlands, the Central Uplands, and the mountain ranges of the Appalachian Mountains in the north-western reaches of the state.

What geographic region is Maine in?

New England region
The Geography of Maine Maine is both the largest and the northernmost state in the New England region, bordered on the west by New Hampshire. It is also the only state that borders exactly one other state.

What does geographic center mean?

center of gravity of
The geographic center of an area may be defined as the center of gravity of the surface, or that point on which the surface of the area would balance if it were a plane of uniform thickness.

Where is the center of the state of Maine?

The geographic center of Maine is located in Piscataquis county, 18 miles north of Dover-Foxcroft.
