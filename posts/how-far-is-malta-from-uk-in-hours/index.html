How far is Malta from UK in hours?

On average, direct flights from the UK to Malta take between 3 hours 10 minutes and 3 hours 30 minutes. It can be cheaper to fly to Malta indirect; flights with stops take from 4 hours 35 minutes to 9 hours or more.

Can you travel to Malta from the UK?

If you arrive from an exempt country or region, you will need to certify you have stayed in that country for at least 2 weeks before you travel to Malta. The Maltese authorities have announced that from 30 June 2021, arrivals from the UK will be required to present proof of full vaccination.

How often does Malta fly to the UK?

Air Malta has exceptionally arranged to maintain a flight to London Heathrow, currently five times a week for a limited period subject to demand. Anyone wishing to leave Malta for the UK on this flight should make an online booking in the normal way.
