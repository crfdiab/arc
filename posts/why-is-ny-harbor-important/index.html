Why is NY harbor important?

Flowing into the Atlantic Ocean, the New York Harbor is a body of water that surrounds Manhattan, Brooklyn, Staten Island and some parts of New Jersey. For centuries, this harbour has played a crucial role in the regional economy and transportation network.

What impact do the bodies of water have on life in New York State?

These inland water bodies serve as the drinking water supplies, provide flood control to protect life and property, and support recreation, tourism, agriculture, fishing, power generation, and manufacturing. These water bodies also provide habitat for aquatic plant and animal life.

Why do we need fresh water in the world?

Clean, fresh water is a limited resource. With all the severe droughts happening in the world, the limited supply of fresh water is becoming one of our most precious resources. Every person on earth needs water to survive. Without it, many of us would get sick and even result in death.

How are people affected by the loss of freshwater?

People are also affected by the loss of freshwater; more than 1 billion people lack access to clean drinking water and 2 billion lack adequate sanitation .This lack of fresh water is one of the most urgent environmental and development issues of the 21st century.

Conserving water means using our water supply wisely and be responsible.
