What causes air weight?

It might not seem like it, but air has weight. This is because the parcel of air is embedded in surrounding air that has the same density (mass per unit volume). But imagine that we remove that parcel of air from the atmosphere…the atmosphere would then weigh very slightly less.

How is the quality of the air measured?

Air quality is measured with the Air Quality Index, or AQI. The AQI works like a thermometer that runs from 0 to 500 degrees. Is there a way to measure air flow?

How is air volume measured in a duct?

Air volume is measured with anemone meter put in the duct. By anemone meter velocity is measured. Multiply cross section area of duct by velocity, gives you volume. Determining air flow is a matter of multiplying the cross sectional area of a duct by the air velocity.
