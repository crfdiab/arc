How do you call a Mexican cell phone from the United States?

Call Mexico from the U.S.

  1. Mobile numbers: Dial + > Dial 52 > Dial the ten-digit mobile number. ( some mobile numbers may require the two or three digit area code)
  2. Landline numbers: Dial + > Dial 52 >Dial the two or three digit area code > Dial the 7 or 8 digit number.
