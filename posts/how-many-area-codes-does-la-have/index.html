How many area codes does LA have?

714 was born, and 805, and then L.A. became the first burg with three area codes within the city limits. Now, nearly 75 years after the birth of 213, Los Angeles County has 10 area codes, eight originally rooted in geography and two that float atop heavily used 310 and 818.

What is Southern California area code?

When first established by AT and the Bell System in 1947, 213 covered southern California including Los Angeles, 415 covered northern/central California including San Francisco and Sacramento, and 916 covered northern California but not including Sacramento.

How are Plus codes used in real life?

Plus Codes are like street addresses for people or places that don’t have one. Instead of addresses with street names and numbers, Plus Codes are based on latitude and longitude, and displayed as numbers and letters. With a Plus Code, people can receive deliveries, access emergency and social services, or just help other people find them.
