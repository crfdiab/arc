How to improve the quality of tourism in India?

Outbreaks of waterborne diseases, such as cholera and dysentery, are common, especially in rural villages with few clean water practices. If India wants to encourage tourism, it needs to act to improve the quality of its air and water so Indians and tourists alike can breathe the air and drink the water freely.
