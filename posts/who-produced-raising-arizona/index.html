What did the critics say about Raising Arizona?

On the film review television show Siskel & Ebert & the Movies, critic Gene Siskel said the film was as “good looking as it is funny” and that “despite some slow patches” he recommended the film, giving it a “thumbs up”. Writing for The New Yorker, Pauline Kael wrote that ” Raising Arizona is no big deal, but it has a rambunctious charm”.
