How many majors are offered at the University of Oregon?

53 majors
University of Oregon offers 79 distinct undergraduate degrees, concentrated into 53 majors within 21 broad fields of study.

How hard is it to get into University of Oregon?

Oregon admissions is somewhat selective with an acceptance rate of 82%. Students that get into Oregon have an average SAT score between 1100-1310 or an average ACT score of 22-28. The regular admissions application deadline for Oregon is January 15.

What can you do at the University of Oregon?

Whether you want to be a researcher or health care provider, an artist or inventor, the University of Oregon offers a variety of degree options if you envision a career in health or fitness. Maybe you’re interested in the building blocks of biochemistry, biology, and neuroscience.

Is the University of Oregon a good choice?

Whenever you walk to class, you will most likely always see at least one person proudly wearing their fraternity or sorority’s Greek letters. If joining Greek Life is a high priority for you, the University of Oregon is a good choice. Fall formal rush is a huge deal here, and there are many more sororities and fraternities every year.
