The Marajó várzea is the inundated land in and around the mouth of the Amazon River in eastern Brazil. The huge river, having completed its 6,500 km journey, empties into the Atlantic Ocean here. The mouth contains numerous islands. The largest is Ilha Marajó at 48,000 km2.
