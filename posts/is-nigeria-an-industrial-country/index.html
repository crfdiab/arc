Is Nigeria an industrial country?

The structure of the Nigerian economy is typical of an underdeveloped country. In contrast, the industrial sector in Nigeria (comprising manufacturing, mining, and utilities) accounts for a tiny proportion of economic activity (6 per cent) while the manufacturing sector contributed only 4 per cent to GDP in 2011.

What are the benefits of industrialization in Nigeria?

BENEFITS OF INDUSTRIALIZATION in NIGERIA
