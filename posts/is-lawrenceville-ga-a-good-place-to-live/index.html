Lawrenceville is in Gwinnett County and is one of the best places to live in Georgia. Living in Lawrenceville offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents rent their homes. In Lawrenceville there are a lot of parks. The public schools in Lawrenceville are highly rated.

Is Lawrenceville GA a suburb of Atlanta?

Lawrenceville is a city and the county seat of Gwinnett County, Georgia, United States. It is a suburb of Atlanta, located approximately 30 miles (50 km) northeast of downtown. As of the 2010 census, the population of Lawrenceville was 28,546.

What’s the distance between Lawrenceville Georgia and Atlanta Georgia?

Distance from Lawrenceville, GA to Atlanta, GA There are 27.05 miles from Lawrenceville to Atlanta in southwest direction and 33 miles (53.11 kilometers) by car, following the I-85 S route.
