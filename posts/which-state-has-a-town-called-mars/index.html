How did Mars PA get its name?

In 1877, Pittsburgh, New Castle, and Lake Erie Railroad organized to build a railroad through Butler County. This was confusing to trainman and an agreement between the railroad and the postal authorities decided on the name Mars, as there was another Overbrook post office in Pennsylvania.
