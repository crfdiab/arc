How many faculties are in Uniport?

twelve faculties
The university now has twelve faculties: Faculty of Humanities. Faculty of Social Sciences. Faculty of Education.

What is the population of Uniport?

According to researchers, the University of Port Harcourt (UNIPORT) has about 44,500 undergraduate students attending it in 2019. Accordingly, they have about 10,000 post graduate students.

What is Uniport known for?

The University of Port Harcourt aims to be ranked amongst the best universities in Africa, renowned for its teaching, research, creativity and innovation.

What is the land mass of Uniport?

… UNIPORT has a total area of 461 ha divided into Choba, Delta and Abuja Park (campus) by three public roads, and is located at coordinates 4 o 52′ 30″ and 4 o 55′ 00″ N, 6 o 54′ 40″ and 6 o 55′ 49″ E ( Eludoyin et al. 2015) within Obio-Akpor local government area of Rivers State, Nigeria.

What is the cut off mark for UNIPORT?

JAMB cutoff mark for UNIPORT is 180. This means that before any aspirant will be offered admission into UNIPORT in 2020, he/she must score at least 180 in JAMB UTME screening. Sometimes, candidates who do not meet up to this cutoff mark are stopped from taking part in AAU’s post UTME examination.
