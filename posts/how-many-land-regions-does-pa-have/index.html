Where are the land regions located in Pennsylvania?

Ridge and Valley — The Capitol Sits Here. The Ridge and Valley land region extends from Pennsylvania’s south-central border, northeast toward New Jersey. It encompasses the Appalachian Mountain range punctuated by gaps and valleys.

What are the three main provinces of Pennsylvania?

Three of the provinces — the Appalachian Plateaus, the Ridge and Valley, and the Piedmont — constitute more than 98 percent of the state.
