What is the most populous province in Indonesia?

West Java
The most populous province is West Java (with more than 43 million people), while the least populous province is that of West Papua in the far eastern region of Indonesia (home to around 761,000 people).

What is the newest province in Indonesia?

Article Indonesia: New Province, Autonomous Regions Planned. (Oct. 24, 2012) Indonesia’s Dewan Perwakilan Rakyat (House of Representatives) has agreed to a plan to form a new province, North Kalimantan. The new province will bring to 34 the total number of provinces in the country.

What is the youngest province in Indonesia?

North Kalimantan
North Kalimantan covers 71,827.3 square kilometres and consists of four regencies and one city. It had a population of 524,656 at the 2010 Census and 701,784 at the 2020 Census, making it the least populous province in Indonesia. Formed on 25 October 2012, it is the youngest province in Indonesia.

Is Java a province?

Java is divided into four administrative provinces: Banten, West Java, Central Java, and East Java, and two special regions, Jakarta and Yogyakarta.

What are the two most popular sports in Indonesia?

Some popular sports in Indonesia are (Soccer) football, volleyball, basketball, badminton, and the native Indonesian martial art pencak silat. Badminton is arguably Indonesia’s most successful sport.
