How big is the land border of Brazil?

Borders of Brazil. The borders of Brazil are the international borders that Brazil shares with neighboring countries. Brazil has borders with ten countries, every country in South America with the exception of Chile and Ecuador, totaling 16,885 kilometres (10,492 mi). Brazil has the world’s third longest land border, behind China and Russia .

Where is the border between Brazil and Uruguay?

Brazilian Island, a river island at the junction of the Quaraí River and the Uruguay River on the border between Argentina, Brazil, and Uruguay is claimed by both Uruguay and Brazil. Brazil has de facto control of it.
