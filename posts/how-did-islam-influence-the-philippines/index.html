How did Islam influence the Philippines?

In 1380 Karim Al Makhdum the first Arabian trader reached the Sulu Archipelago and Jolo in the Philippines and established Islam in the country through trade in several regions of the island. In 1390 the Minangkabau’s Prince Rajah Baguinda and his followers preached Islam on the islands.

How did the Spaniards influence the Filipino way of life?

Spain not only brought the Catholic religion to the Philippine islands, it also brought with it its culture and cuisine. When the Spaniards arrived, they brought with them ingredients and labor-intensive cooking methods that were unknown in the Philippines.
