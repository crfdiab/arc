Where do I spend gems in Grand Chase?

Gems are used for Powering-up Necklaces or Anklets. They can be bought in the shop under the Academy tab for 1000 GP each. They can also be used to feed pets. Another use for them are for unlocking certain characters with their cash mission or other quests.

Who is the traitor in Grand Chase?

Supposedly, there is a traitor among the Twelve Disciples but the identity was not disclosed. While Amy was denoted as the traitor, no act of betrayal was proven and Amy herself told the truth of the Prophecy to the Grand Chase, declaring her innocence.

How do you use SA in Grand Chase?

Pressing Alt will activate the Dodge System, which saps a little bit of the SA bar; if the evasive maneuver is used, more SA bar is consumed in the process.

How do you get a crest in Grand Chase?

Crests are equipped by going to the equip tab, and then clicking Collection that is directly under the Equip button. Players then click on the Title tab to view all available crests. Those without a button are unattainable. Those with the Quest button means they require Champion Mode runs to obtain.

How do you Fusion a Grand Chase Mobile?

The second requirement in Grand Chase Mobile fusion process is both the heroes should be on the same Evo rank; 3-star+3-star. You can not fuse a 3-star hero with 4-star hero. How to fuse -> Tap the bag icon at the top-right corner -> tap the fuse button.

Is Grand Chase Mobile dead?

As of April 15, the game ceased development and terminated its service worldwide, with North America being the last foreign server to close in 2015. The mobile sequel Grand Chase Dimensional Chaser was launched in January 2018.

How do you get Champion Mode tickets in Grand Chase?

A Ticket is obtained either through daily attendance(as well as the bonus “perfect” weekly attendance),or through the shop, under Academy, for 200,000 GP for 1 ticket or 1000 Cash for 5.

How do you awaken GrandChase?

Via Awakening. Awakening replaces a Gold ☆ with a Pink ☆. Once a hero reaches 6☆ +12, awakening is possible. A hero can awaken up to 6 times. To awaken, one must have the same 6☆ +12 unawakened Hero, or a Shiny of the same rank.
