Is Ocean City MD expensive?

The cost of staying in Ocean City is higher than the average city. On average hotels are less expensive than vacation rentals. Luxury vacation rentals are more expensive in Ocean City due to very high property costs.

What is the best time to go to Ocean City Maryland?

The best time to visit Ocean City is from late May to early September, just be prepared for average highs in the mid-80s and a little humidity. There will also be a lot of crowds, and hotel rates that are double the cost of visiting in spring, so consider booking several months in advance.

How long is the ride from Philadelphia to Ocean City Maryland?

How long is the train journey from Philadelphia to Ocean City? The distance between Philadelphia and Ocean City is approximately 112 miles, or 180 kilometers. The average train journey between these two cities takes 5 hours and 43 minutes, although the absolute fastest you could get there is 4 hours and 41 minutes.

How much money should you take to Ocean City Maryland?

How much money will you need for your trip to Ocean City? You should plan to spend around $128 per day on your vacation in Ocean City, which is the average daily price based on the expenses of other visitors. Past travelers have spent, on average, $39 on meals for one day and $38 on local transportation.

Can you swim in Ocean City MD in June?

Is it warm enough to swim in Ocean City in June? Average water temperature in Ocean City in June is 68.9°F and therefore suitable for comfortable swimming. Average high air temperature in Ocean City in June is 81°F, and average low temperature is 61°F.

Is there a bus from Philadelphia to Ocean City Maryland?

There is usually only one bus per day from Philadelphia to Ocean City. However, it is not a direct bus, so you will have to get off your original vehicle to change buses during the trip.
