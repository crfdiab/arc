What did Princes Street Gardens used to be?

Princes Street Gardens was created by draining Edinburgh’s largest loch, the Nor Loch. Nor Loch, which was first a marshland on the north side of Edinburgh had initially been used as a natural Medieval defence along with Edinburgh Castle.

Why is the Scott Monument black?

Over the years, the original pale sandstone and white marble had darkened to sooty black as a result of air pollution. Now Scott’s statue is again white and the new stonework is the original light colour. It was decided not to clean all the sandstone, to prevent further damage.

Can you go up the Scott Monument?

If you want to climb to the top of the tower you’ll have to go up 287 steps, which are divided into four levels where you can stop for a while and explore if you wish. Can u drink in the streets in Edinburgh?
