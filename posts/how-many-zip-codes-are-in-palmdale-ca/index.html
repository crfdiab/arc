How dangerous is Palmdale CA?

The chance of becoming a victim of either violent or property crime in Palmdale is 1 in 53. Based on FBI crime data, Palmdale is not one of the safest communities in America. Relative to California, Palmdale has a crime rate that is higher than 47% of the state’s cities and towns of all sizes.

What is Palmdale CA known for?

Separated from Los Angeles by the San Gabriel Mountains, Palmdale lies north of Los Angeles and is nicknamed the ‘aerospace capital of America’ due to its main industry being that of aerospace technology.
