What happened to minyards?

The company had 5,200 employees and an estimated sales of $900 million in 2004. It went through a difficult time under Q Investments of Fort Worth and ended up closing and selling stores. San Angelo grocers Ray and Leticia Schalek acquired what was left of Minyard in 2011.

What does H-E-B grocery store stand for?
