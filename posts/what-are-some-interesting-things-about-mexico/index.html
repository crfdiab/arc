What is the geography of the Republic of Mexico?

Geography The Republic of Mexico’s 31 statesand one federal district covers almost 780,000 square mi (2 million square km) making it the world’s eight largest nation. The country curves from northwest to southeast, narrowing to the Isthmus of Tehuantepecin the south and then continuing northeast to the YucatanPeninsula.
