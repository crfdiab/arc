What was king George V real name?

George V/Full name

George V, in full George Frederick Ernest Albert, (born June 3, 1865, London, England—died January 20, 1936, Sandringham, Norfolk), king of the United Kingdom from 1910 to 1936, the second son of Prince Albert Edward, later King Edward VII.

Was king George V mean to his kids?

George V was known to have a slightly tense relationship with his children, due to his strict nature. His fourth child, Prince Henry, went as far as to call him a “terrible father”, according to historian Hugo Vickers.

Who did king George V descend from?

The second son of Prince Edward of Wales (later King Edward VII) and Alexandra of Denmark, and the grandson of Queen Victoria, George was born in 1865 and embarked on a naval career before becoming heir to the throne in 1892 when his older brother, Edward, died of pneumonia.
