When does an ecosystem go into an ecological imbalance?

If something happens in an ecosystem, it can shift from a state of balance to a state of imbalance. Ecological imbalance is when a natural or human-caused disturbance disrupts the natural balance of an ecosystem. A disturbance is any change that causes a disruption in the balance of an ecosystem.

How does deforestation affect an unbalanced ecosystem?

Deforestation impacts changes in temperature causing the forest floor to become dryer. The plant matter that was once there before deforestation helped keep the ecosystem together but due to deforestation, the lack of trees increases wind and flood rains to impact the forest floor which raises the chance of erosion.
