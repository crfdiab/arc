Who started penny press?

Benjamin H. Day
ABOUT THE FOUNDER • The Penny Press is generally considered to have started in 1833, when Benjamin H. Day founded The Sun, a New York City newspaper. The Sun was the first popular penny paper.

What were the names of the two most popular penny papers?
