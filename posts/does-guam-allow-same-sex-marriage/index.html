Does Guam allow same sex marriage?

Same-sex marriage in Guam has been licensed and recognized since June 9, 2015, following a ruling of the District Court of Guam on June 5, 2015, that held the territory’s prohibition of same-sex marriage unconstitutional.

What was the percentage of support for same sex marriage in 2004?

In Pew Research Center polling in 2004, Americans opposed same-sex marriage by a margin of 60% to 31%. Support for same-sex marriage has steadily grown over the past 15 years. And today, support for same-sex marriage remains near its highest point since Pew Research Center began polling on this issue.

How did the public opinion change on same sex marriage?

As countries began to legalize same-sex partnerships, public opinion, particularly in Europe, began to shift in favour of full marriage rights for same-sex unions.
