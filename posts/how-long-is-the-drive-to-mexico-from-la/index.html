What do you need to drive your own car in Mexico?

Whether you’re renting a car in Mexico or driving your own across the border, there are several documents that are needed to operate a motor vehicle in Mexico. To drive your own car into Mexico, you’ll need a tourist card and a temporary vehicle importation permit, which you can get on arrival at the border.
