Where is the boundary between Delaware and New Jersey?

The boundary between Delaware and New Jersey north of 39° 30′ north latitude is the east bank of the Delaware River. The boundary between Delaware and New Jersey, south of a certain point, is the west bank of the Delaware River rather than the thread of the channel.
