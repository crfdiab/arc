How many airports are in the world?

According to the Airports Council International (ACI) World Airport Traffic Report, there are currently 17,678 commercial airports in the world, in other words those which receive airliners, cargo and business aircraft.

Which country has the most airports in the world?

Usually the world’s largest domestic market, the United States has by far the most airports of any country in the world. According to the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), there are 19,633 airports across the country.
