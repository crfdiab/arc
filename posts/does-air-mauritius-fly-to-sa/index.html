Can I emigrate to Mauritius?

Citizens from most countries don’t need to have an entry visa for Mauritius. If you plan to live and work in Mauritius, you must obtain a work permit. After three years, you can apply for a permanent residence permit that will allow you to stay for ten years on the island.
