Where is the city of Afton, MN located?

The City of Afton is located approximately fifteen miles east of downtown St. Paul, south of I-94 on the eastern border of Minnesota.

What is the phone number for Afton Chemical Corporation?

Is the Main Street Museum in Afton NY open?

The Museum is open, from Memorial Day through October, on Saturdays, from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. The Museum is located at 116 Main Street, Afton, NY 13730. From November through May we are only open by appointment.

When did the Afton Historical Society get established?

The Afton Historical Society was established in 1986. It was the successor to the Jericho Historical Society and the Isabelle House Association. Our goal is to preserve, protect, and promote historic culture in the Town/Village of Afton through our research, website, publications, educational programs, and community outreach.
