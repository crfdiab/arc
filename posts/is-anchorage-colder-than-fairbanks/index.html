That’s true, Fairbanks is MUCH colder than Anchorage in Winter — conversely Fairbanks is warmer in Summer than Anchorage. Both phenomena because Anchorage is located next to an arm of the North Pacific ocean — Anchorage is located on the SE shore of Cook Inlet, a Pacific ocean arm of the Gulf of Alaska.
