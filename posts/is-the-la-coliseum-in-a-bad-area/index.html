Is there parking at Exposition Park in Los Angeles?

There are a limited number of public parking spaces in Exposition Park. For Rams and USC games, parking is by permit only; there are no cash lots. For all other events, public parking sells out quickly and rates may vary per event. Entrances to Exposition Park are located at MLK & Hoover and 39th & Figueroa.
