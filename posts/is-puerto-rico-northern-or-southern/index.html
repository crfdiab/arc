Is Puerto Rico Northern or Southern?

Puerto Rico is located above the equator, meaning it is situated in the northern hemisphere. The Caribbean island of Puerto Rico is also positioned in the western hemisphere. As part of the Greater Antilles, Puerto Rico is the smallest of the islands in this region of the Caribbean.

Is Puerto Rico Western or Eastern?

The geography of Puerto Rico consists of an archipelago located between the Caribbean Sea and the North Atlantic Ocean, east of the Dominican Republic, west of the Virgin Islands and north of Venezuela. The main island of Puerto Rico is the smallest and most eastern of the Greater Antilles.
