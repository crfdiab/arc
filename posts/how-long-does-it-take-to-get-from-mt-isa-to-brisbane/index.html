Is there a train from Brisbane to Mt Isa?

No, there is no direct train from Brisbane to Mount Isa station. However, there are services departing from Brisbane Roma Street and arriving at Mount Isa station via Townsville station. The journey, including transfers, takes approximately 39h 48m.

What is the population of Mount Isa?

approximately 21,000
Welcome to Mount Isa, the traditional lands of the Kalkadoon and Indjilandji people. Our city is located in north-west Queensland on the banks of the Leichhardt River. With a population of approximately 21,000, it is the major service centre for the area.

What is the best way to drive from Brisbane to Melbourne?

There is no pleasant way to drive Brisbane-Melbourne in 3 days. The Hume Freeway is probably the quickest drive with freeeway most of the way. Possibly more scenic is the New England Hwy and Newell Hwy to Seymour, then Hume Hwy into Melbourne.
