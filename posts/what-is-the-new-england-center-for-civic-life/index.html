What is the New England Center for civic life?

The New England Center for Civic Life is dedicated to the teaching, practice and study of deliberative democracy. The Center’s initiatives and research are based on the premise that in order to foster strong communities, based on principles of inclusiveness and equity, individuals must engage in thoughtful and civil dialogue.

When did the Flynt center of early New England Life Open?

The Flynt Center of Early New England Life, which opened in 1998, provides a 27,000 square-foot, state-of-the-art facility containing climate-controlled exhibition galleries, visible storage area, and collection storage for the museum’s most sensitive and highly valued collections.

Where is the New England life insurance company?

New England Life Insurance Company is a provider of life insurance and annuities and has its home office in Boston, Massachusetts.

Why was New England important in the Industrial Revolution?

By the 1840s, New England was the center of the American anti-slavery movement and was the leading force in American literature and higher education. It was at the center of the Industrial Revolution in America, with many textile mills and machine shops operating by 1830.
