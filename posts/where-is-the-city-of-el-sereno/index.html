Where is the city of El Sereno?

Los Angeles
The El Sereno – Berkshire Craftsman District HPOZ is located in the easternmost part of Los Angeles. It is bordered by the City of South Pasadena to the north and the City of Alhambra to the east.

Is El Sereno considered East LA?

El Sereno is a Los Angeles neighborhood in the Eastside Los Angeles region of Los Angeles County, California.

What district is El Sereno?

Council District 14
The El Sereno area of Council District 14 covers the 90032 ZIP Code, including the neighborhoods of Rose Hills, University Hills, and Hillside Village; as well as the adjacent neighborhoods of Hermon and Monterey Hills. The majority of El Sereno was annexed to the city in 1915 as part of the Bairdstown Addition.
