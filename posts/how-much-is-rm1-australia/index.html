What kind of currency is RP?

Indonesian rupiah
The Indonesian rupiah (IDR) is the official currency of Indonesia, a Southest Asian country made up of thousands of small islands. IDR is the unique currency code of the Indonesian rupiah under ISO code 4217. The rupiah is made up of 100 sen, and is often presented with the symbol or abbreviation Rp.
