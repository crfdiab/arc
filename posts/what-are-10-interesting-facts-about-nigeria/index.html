Does Nigeria specialize in anything?

Nigeria has a high level of specialization in Scrap Vessels (213), Flexible Metal Tubing (133), Tin Ores (52), Tanned Sheep Hides (25.7), and Tanned Goat Hides (24.9). Specialization is measured using RCA, an index that takes the ratio between Nigeria observed and expected exports in each product.

What is the city of Lagos known for?

Lagos, Nigeria is the hub of business activities in Nigeria. Its known for its busy lifestyle where everyone is rushing to meet deadlines. It’s a city for only the smart and fast and streetwise.

Which is the most populous city in Nigeria?

Lagos is Nigeria’s most populous city and served as her capital up until 19191 when Abuja became the seat of power, however, Lagos has continued to be the Country’s most popular City. It is largely famous for its Nightlife; arguably the most vibrant on the continent.
