Why does the Pentagon have a Washington DC address?

The Pentagon has a Washington, D.C. mailing address. When the Pentagon site was chosen in 1941, it occupied in part the site of the old Washington-Hoover Airport, which had closed after the opening of National Airport. Back in 1929, Washington Airport had sued a contractor building the Mount Vernon Memorial Highway.
