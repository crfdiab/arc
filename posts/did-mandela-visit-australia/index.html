Did Mandela visit Australia?

BORN a century ago today, Mandela was a hero to millions of people worldwide but he disappointed indigenous Australians when he visited in 1990. High on his list of countries to visit was Australia. This was partly because we had been among the strongest campaigners against apartheid.

What is Nelson Mandela fighting for?

Former South African president and civil rights advocate Nelson Mandela dedicated his life to fighting for equality—and ultimately helped topple South Africa’s racist system of apartheid. His accomplishments are now celebrated each year on July 18, Nelson Mandela International Day.

Is Nelson Mandela from Cape Town?

Cape Town is home to South Africa’s parliament, and it is from here that Mandela ran the country as president. Between 1994 and 1999, he served as South Africa’s first democratically elected ruler from this building, and it’s open to the public for free tours.
