What state can you find the Grand Canyon National Park?

Arizona
Grand Canyon is in the northwest corner of Arizona, close to the borders of Utah and Nevada. The Colorado River, which flows through the canyon, drains water from seven states, but the feature we know as Grand Canyon is entirely in Arizona.

What is the closest city to Grand Canyon National Park?

Flagstaff
Flagstaff is the closest major city near the Grand Canyon, located about 80 miles from both the south and east entrance stations to the South Rim.

Where is the Grand Canyon national park located?

75. Inscription. 1979 (3rd session) Grand Canyon National Park, located in northwestern Arizona, is the 15th site in the United States to have been named as a national park. The park’s central feature is the Grand Canyon, a gorge of the Colorado River, which is often considered one of the Wonders of the World.

Where is the Grand Canyon in Flagstaff AZ?

North of Interstate 40 and the cities of Williams and Flagstaff, AZ. East of the Navajo and Hopi Indian Reservations, and south of the Utah state line. The Colorado River is shown through the entire 277 mile transect of Grand Canyon National Park.
