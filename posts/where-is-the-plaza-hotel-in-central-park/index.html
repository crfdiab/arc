Where is the Plaza Hotel in Central Park?

It is on the western side of Grand Army Plaza, just west of Fifth Avenue, between 58th Street and Central Park South. The Plaza Hotel is named for Grand Army Plaza, which in turn is at the southeastern corner of Central Park.
