Is there a ferry from UK to Oslo?

By ferry from the UK There are currently no car ferries from the UK to Norway; the nearest you’ll get is Esbjerg in Denmark, about 900km (around 10hr) by road from Oslo, with DFDS Seaways (wdfdsseaways.co.uk) from Harwich.

Which airport is closest to Oslo?

Gardermoen
Oslo Lufthavn is located at Gardermoen (50 km from Oslo, see map) and is Norway’s main airport. If you are flying to Oslo you can also use Torp Sandefjord Airport (110 km from Oslo, see map). Many low-cost airlines fly into Torp.

Which airport do you fly into for Rio de Janeiro?

Rio de Janeiro–Galeão International Airport
Rio de Janeiro–Galeão International Airport (GIG) is the major airport in the city and offers passenger and cargo flights throughout Brazil and around the world. The airport has two runways and two passenger terminals, which have been expanded due to the popularity of Rio de Janeiro as a vacation destination.
