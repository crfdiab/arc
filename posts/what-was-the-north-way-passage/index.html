What was the North way passage?

The Northwest Passage (NWP) is the sea route between the Atlantic and Pacific oceans through the Arctic Ocean, along the northern coast of North America via waterways through the Canadian Arctic Archipelago. Arctic sea ice decline has rendered the waterways more navigable for ice navigation.

What is Canada doing to assert sovereignty in the North?

In 2010, the federal government announced plans to build a fleet of six to eight “ice-capable, Arctic/Offshore Patrol Ships,” to enforce Canada’s sovereignty on its coasts, including the Arctic.

What was the story of a passage to India?

E.M. Forster’s multi-layered masterpiece is on the surface the story of a young woman and her passage through India. On another level it’s the story of India’s independence from British rule.
