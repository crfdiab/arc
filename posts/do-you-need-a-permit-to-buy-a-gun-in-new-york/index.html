Do you need a permit to buy a gun in New York?

State permit to purchase long guns: New York does not issue a state permit to buy long guns. State permits to purchase handguns: New York issues a state permit to purchase handguns. State permit to open carry: New York is a non-permissive state that prohibits the open carry of firearms.

Can a felon own a gun in New York?

A: No. If you have an assault weapon, you can register it with the State Police. Under state and federal law, some people are not allowed to possess a weapon, such as convicted felons, individuals who have been involuntarily committed, or individuals currently under an order of protection.

How does the New York gun bill of sale work?

The New York Gun Bill of Sale is used as a legal document that is will prove the legal sale, purchase and change of ownership of a firearm.

Is it illegal to sell ammunition in New York?

It is unlawful for any dealer in firearms to sell any ammunition designed exclusively for use in a pistol or revolver to any person who is not authorized to possess a pistol or revolver. NY Penal Law § 270.00.

How much does it cost to register a gun in New York?

New York requires registration of handguns. Long guns do not need to be registered. It is illegal to own an unregistered handgun. It costs $3 to register a firearm.

How to buy a gun in Long Island NY?

How often do you have to renew your gun license in New York?

New York is one of a small number of states where local governments can enact legislation that varies from state law. For instance, in New York City, pistol licenses must be renewed every three years for a fee of $340.
