Why is Hawaii significant to the US?

A key provisioning spot for American whaling ships, fertile ground for American protestant missionaries, and a new source of sugar cane production, Hawaii’s economy became increasingly integrated with the United States.

Is Hawaii also a country?

Hawaii, constituent state of the United States of America. Hawaii (Hawaiian: Hawai’i) became the 50th U.S. state on August 21, 1959. Hawaii is a group of volcanic islands in the central Pacific Ocean. The capital is Honolulu, located on the island of Oahu.
