Which country is known as Thousand lakes?

Finland
The forested landscape is dotted with patches of water – or, in some areas, vice versa – so numerous they have earned Finland the nickname “the land of the thousand lakes”. In fact, the moniker is an understatement, as there are a total of 188 000 lakes in Finland.

What is the largest country that borders Norway?

Sweden
Norway shares its longest border with Sweden. Norway has a land border that stretches 1,568 miles long. The country shares its land border with three countries; Sweden, Russia, and Finland where Sweden shares the longest portion of the border, while Russia shares the shortest.

Why are there so many lakes in Norway?

It will not be superficial to say that Norway is the land of lakes. There are almost half a million lakes in Norway which are identified. The total estimated area covered by lakes is estimated as 17,100 km square. It is said that the most of the lakes are evolved due to glacier erosion.
