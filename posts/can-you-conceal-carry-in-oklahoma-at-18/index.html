Can you conceal carry in Oklahoma at 18?

Oklahoma has permitless carry. It allows anyone who is at least 21 years old (or at least 18 years old and is a member of the military or honorably discharged veteran) that can legally own a firearm to openly or concealed carry without a license. Licenses are not available for non-residents.
