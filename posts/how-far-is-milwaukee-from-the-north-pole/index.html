Are snowy owls in Wisconsin?

Snowy owls are back in Wisconsin, and spotting them is a social-distance friendly activity. Snowy owls spend their summers nesting on the tundra north of the Arctic circle. Come winter, some migrate south into southern Canada and the northern United States, including Wisconsin.
