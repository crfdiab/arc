What part of Tennessee is Graceland in?

Memphis, Tennessee
Graceland is Elvis’ actual home, and it is situated on a 13.8 acre estate in Memphis, Tennessee. Since the passing of the legendary music icon, Graceland has been under the ownership of Elvis’ daughter Lisa Marie Presley. The complete address of the Graceland mansion is 3764 Elvis Presley Boulevard, Memphis, Tennessee.
