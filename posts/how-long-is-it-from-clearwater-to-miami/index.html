How long is it from Clearwater to Miami?

Driving directions from Clearwater to Miami There is a 279 miles distance between Clearwater and Miami. Driving to Miami takes about 5 hours under normal driving conditions with reasonable traffic and no excessive lines at the border crossing, making it the fastest way between the two cities.

How nice is Clearwater?

Recently voted the No. 1 beach in the United States by TripAdvisor, Clearwater Beach on Florida’s west coast is as family-friendly as it gets. With sugar-white sand, many low-key restaurants, a ton of activities, huge playground and nightly shows, your family will absolutely love vacationing here.

How much would a trip to Clearwater cost?

The average price of a 7-day trip to Clearwater Beach is $1,606 for a solo traveler, $2,884 for a couple, and $5,407 for a family of 4. Clearwater Beach hotels range from $86 to $394 per night with an average of $154, while most vacation rentals will cost $240 to $560 per night for the entire home.
