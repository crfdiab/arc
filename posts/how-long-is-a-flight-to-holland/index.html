How long is the flight from Atlanta to Scotland?

Flying time from Atlanta, GA to Scotland The total flight duration from Atlanta, GA to Scotland is 8 hours, 31 minutes. This assumes an average flight speed for a commercial airliner of 500 mph, which is equivalent to 805 km/h or 434 knots. It also adds an extra 30 minutes for take-off and landing. Your exact time may vary depending on wind speeds.
