What Wyoming city is closest to Denver?

Cheyenne Its population is just under 60,000 people, making it the most populated place in the state. In addition, it’s one of the only towns in Wyoming that is close to large cities. Fort Collins and Denver, Colorado are only 46 and 101 miles away, respectively.

How far is Cheyenne from the Colorado border?

By car[edit] Cheyenne is located at the crossroads of Interstates 80 and 25 in southeastern Wyoming, about 40 miles west of the Nebraska border, 180 miles south of Casper, 50 miles east of Laramie, and 8 miles north of the Colorado border. Cheyenne is 90 miles directly north of Denver.

Is Wyoming close to Colorado?

Distance from Colorado to Wyoming is 411 kilometers. The air travel (bird fly) shortest distance between Colorado and Wyoming is 411 km= 255 miles. If you travel with an airplane (which has average speed of 560 miles) from Colorado to Wyoming, It takes 0.46 hours to arrive.
