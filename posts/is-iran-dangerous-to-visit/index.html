Is Iran dangerous to visit?

Iran is generally a very safe place to travel, so much so that many travellers describe it as the ‘safest country I’ve ever been to’, or ‘much safer than travelling in Europe’. What does Iran do for entertainment?

Entertainment in Iran: Zurkhaneh Another popular type of entertainment in Iran is zurkhaneh. The word is translated as “house of power”. This is a traditional sports arena, where men in uniform perform strength exercises. Zurkhaneh is accompanied by loud sounds of a drum and a traditional song.

What are the best things to do in Tehran, Iran?

Right in the heart of the city, the Grand Bazaar is an essential visit for any tourist in Tehran. The bazaar is absolutely huge. There are banks, restaurants, tea houses, stunning mausoleums and mosques and it covers over 6 miles (10 km).
