What is the name of the largest sea south of France in between Europe and Africa?

Mediterranean Sea
The Mediterranean Sea is a sea connected to the Atlantic Ocean, surrounded by the Mediterranean Basin and almost completely enclosed by land: on the north by Western and Southern Europe and Anatolia, on the south by North Africa, and on the east by the Levant.

Which is the sea that separates Europe from Asia?

The Mediterranean Sea – The breathtaking Mediterranean Sea separates Europe and Asia, with an impressive coastline spanning more than 28,000 miles and 22 countries. The sea is connected to the Atlantic Ocean via the narrow Strait of Gibraltar, which flows between Spain and Morocco. Most of the sea is landlocked, which limits its tides.
