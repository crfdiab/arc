How many hours does it take from Canada to New Zealand?

Flying nonstop from Vancouver Airport (YVR) to Auckland Airport (AKL), the Vancouver to New Zealand flight time is 14 hours and 35 minutes. The closest gateway from Canada to this land of magic beauty, Vancouver is the only direct route and Air New Zealand the only carrier to operate it.

Can I travel to New Zealand from Canada now?

Since October 1, 2019, Canadians entering New Zealand without a visa need to get an electronic travel authority before their arrival, even if they are transiting to another country.

Do Canadian citizens need a visa for New Zealand?

Canadian nationals are required to have an NZeTA in order to travel to New Zealand without a visa for touristic and business purposes. The Electronic Travel Authority is also mandatory for Canadians to transit through the country en route to another destination.

How long is the flight from Toronto to Australia?

The total flight duration from Toronto, Canada to Australia is 21 hours, 6 minutes. This assumes an average flight speed for a commercial airliner of 500 mph, which is equivalent to 805 km/h or 434 knots. It also adds an extra 30 minutes for take-off and landing.
