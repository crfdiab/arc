Where in America is New Orleans?

Where is New Orleans? The city of New Orleans is located in the southern US state of Louisiana, on the banks of the Mississippi River. Straddling Lake Pontchartrain to the west and Lake Borgne to the east, New Orleans is approximately 80 miles south-west of the Louisiana capital, Baton Rouge.

Where is the southern boundary of New Orleans?

Lake Pontchartrain is connected by The Rigolets channel to Lake Borgne on the east (and thence to the gulf), and the southern boundary of New Orleans is made up of St. Bernard parish and, again, the Mississippi River.
