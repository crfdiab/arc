What does happy holidays really mean?

Why is it called the holidays?

The word holiday comes from the Old English word hāligdæg (hālig “holy” + dæg “day”). The word originally referred only to special religious days. The modern use varies geographically. In North America, it means any dedicated day or period of celebration.
