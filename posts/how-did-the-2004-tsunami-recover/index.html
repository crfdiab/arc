How did the 2004 tsunami recover?

Rebuilding and replanting Home-building was a major component of the recovery effort and 140,000 homes were built. Over half those living in heavily-damaged communities have benefited directly from housing assistance and 90% of households in those areas own their homes today.

Why did so many people die in the Indian Ocean tsunami?
