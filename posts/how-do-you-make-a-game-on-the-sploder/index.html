How do you make a game on the Sploder?

Making games on Sploder works pretty much the same way whether you are creating a shooter, platformer or 3d game. Simply log in, click “Make a game” and then choose the type of game you want to make. Once the creator loads, you can design your playfield and drag and drop objects onto it to create your game.

Is Sploder dead?

How can I play Flash games after 2020?
