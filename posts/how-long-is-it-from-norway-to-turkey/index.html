How long is it from Norway to Turkey?

The air travel (bird fly) shortest distance between Norway and Turkey is 3,030 km= 1,883 miles. If you travel with an airplane (which has average speed of 560 miles) from Norway to Turkey, It takes 3.36 hours to arrive.

How do you get from Norway to Turkey?

In order to apply for a residence permit to Norway from Turkey, you must either be a citizen of Turkey or have a residence permit in Turkey. Turkish citizens submit their applications at one of the Visa Application Centers in Ankara, Antalya or Izmir, and not at the Embassy.
