What kind of food do Punjabis eat in India?

Punjabis like to eat foods cooked inside a tandoor. This style was basically popular in India after the partition when Punjabis resettled in Delhi. In rural areas of Punjab, the tandoor is called “Kath tandoors”. Wheat is mainly produced in Punjab as it is the major producer of wheat.
