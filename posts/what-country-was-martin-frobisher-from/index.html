Who was the mother of Martin Frobisher’s children?

Martin Frobisher’s mother was Margaret, néeYorke, of Gowthwaite. She bore at least five children who survived into adulthood; these were, in probable order of birth, John, David, Martin, Jane and Margaret.

Where did Sir Martin Frobisher go on his voyages?

Sir Martin Frobisher. Frobisher went on voyages to the Guinea coast of Africa in 1553 and 1554, and during the 1560s he preyed on French shipping in the English Channel under a privateering license from the English crown; he was arrested several times on charges of piracy but never brought to trial.

Where did Sir Martin Frobisher discover Baffin Island?

Sir Martin Frobisher, mariner, privateer, explorer (born ca. 1535 near Wakefield, England; died 22 November 1594 in Plymouth, England). Frobisher discovered the bay now named for him on Baffin Island, but was deceived by the pyrites, which he took for gold.

What did Martin Frobisher do on his voyage?
