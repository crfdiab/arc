Why is Grand Portage important?

The Grand Portage was a vital part of both American Indian and fur trade transportation routes because of the area’s geology, topography, natural resources, and strategic location between the upper Great Lakes and the interior of western Canada.

Where is the Grand Portage State Forest in Minnesota?

The Grand Portage State Forest is a state forest located near the community of Hovland in Cook County, in extreme northeastern Minnesota.
