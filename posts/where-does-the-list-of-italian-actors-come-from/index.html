Who was the first Italian actress to speak Italian?

CLAUDIA CARDINALE Born in Tunisia in 1938 from Sicilian parents, Claudia Cardinale grew up speaking French, Arabic and Sicilian and did not learn Italian until she began to be cast for Italian films. However, in her first Italian films, she was dubbed, reportedly because of her deep hoarse voice and heavy French accent.
