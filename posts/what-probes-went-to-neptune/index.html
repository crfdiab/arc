How did Voyager 1 find out about Neptune?

Voyager returned a wealth of information about Neptune and its moons-and confirmed evidence the giant world had faint rings like the other gas planets. Scientists also use the Hubble Space Telescope and powerful ground-based telescopes to gather more information about this distant planet

Are there any plans to explore Neptune’s moons?

Two different proposals were put forward in 2005 for unmanned spacecraft to explore Neptune and its moons, especially Triton. These studies are part of NASA’s Vision Mission program to develop long-term space exploration goals.
