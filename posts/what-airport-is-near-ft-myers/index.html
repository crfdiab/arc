What airport is near Ft Myers?

Ft. Myers
The nearest airport to Fort Myers is Ft. Myers (RSW) Airport which is 8.4 miles away. Other nearby airports include Sarasota/Bradenton (SRQ) (69.1 miles), Tampa (TPA) (103.4 miles), Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International (FLL) (112.3 miles) and Miami (MIA) (113.1 miles).

Is Fort Lauderdale near Ft Myers?

What is the distance between Fort Lauderdale and Fort Myers? The distance between Fort Lauderdale and Fort Myers is 111 miles. The road distance is 138.5 miles.

How far apart is Fort Myers and Fort Lauderdale?

Distance from Fort Lauderdale, FL to Fort Myers, FL There are 113.20 miles from Fort Lauderdale to Fort Myers in northwest direction and 141 miles (226.92 kilometers) by car, following the I-75 N route. Fort Lauderdale and Fort Myers are 2 hours 11 mins far apart, if you drive non-stop .

Is Fort Myers a good place to live?

“Fort Myers is one of the most desirable places on Florida’s Gulf Coast to live, work, and play,” says Tracey Tucker of Montgomery Real Estate. “The city has a relaxed atmosphere, offers a reasonable cost of living, and is characterized by a diverse range of college students, young families, and retirees.”

How long is ferry from Ft Myers to Key West?

3.5 hours
It’s not just a way to get from place to place though – it’s an adventure. During your 3.5 hours on the ferry you’ll be on the open water in the Gulf of Mexico, making for some incredible views, especially on the Key West–to–Fort Myers ferry, where you’ll be treated to a gorgeous sunset.

Is it expensive to live in Fort Myers Florida?

Cost Of Living In Fort Myers, FL According to Bestplaces.net, the cost of living index in Fort Myers, FL is 96.2, which is lower than the Florida average of 102.8, and the US average of 100. However, the cost of healthcare (117.3) and groceries (103.2) are both significantly higher than the US and Florida averages.
