What are some effects caused by urbanization?

Poor air and water quality, insufficient water availability, waste-disposal problems, and high energy consumption are exacerbated by the increasing population density and demands of urban environments.

What is urbanization health?

Healthy urbanization is the desired direction of urban health systems development that aims to protect and promote public health rather than threaten or erode health of individuals and communities in urban areas.
