Which is the second most populated country in Europe?

Gibraltar The British overseas territory of the Iberian Peninsula is the second most densely populated country in Europe. Gibraltar covers an area of 2.6 square miles and has a population of more than 32,000 people resulting in population density of more than 11,000 people per square mile.
