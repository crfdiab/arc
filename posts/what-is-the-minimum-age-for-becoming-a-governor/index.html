What is the minimum age for appointment as a governor a 35 years B 40 years C 25 years D 30 years?

The qualifications of a person to be the governor are that He/she: must be a citizen of India. must at least be 35 years of age. must not be a member of either house of the parliament or house of the state legislature.

What is the minimum age to be the Governor of a state?

According to the Constitution of India, the qualifications to be met by a person to be appointed as a Governor of the State are: He or she must be a citizen of India. He or she must have completed 35 years of age minimum. He or she must not hold any other office of profit.

How to become governor of a state in India?

2.A citizen of India who has completed the age of 35 years can become a governor (Article 157). But he should not be an MP or MLA and he should not hold any office of profit (Article 158). He should not belong to the state where he is appointed so that he doesn’t get involved in the local politics (this is violated in some cases) .

Is there retirement age for governor in India?

There is no retirement age of the Governor, as he or she stays in office for a fixed term. There is no provision for a Governor to be impeached from office, unlike that of a President. A Governor of a particular state is entitled to a fixed pension, as per the Constitution of India.
