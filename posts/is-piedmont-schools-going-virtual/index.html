What is the best school district in Oklahoma?

Deer Creek School District
Deer Creek School District is the best school system in Oklahoma. Comprising nine school buildings serving an enrollment of over 6900, DCSD boasts a graduation rate of 96%, which is over 13 points higher than the state average. Placing 2nd is Public Bixby Schools, which consists of nine schools in the Bixby area.

What GPA do you need for Piedmont College?

3.0 GPA
Apply and be accepted in the same manner as a regular undergraduate student, meeting the specific requirements for DCE students. These requirements are: Minimum 3.0 GPA. SAT: Minimum 1080 Total score, 530 Math and 550 Evidence-Based Reading and Writing.

What GPA do you need to get into Piedmont College?

3.4
With a GPA of 3.4, Piedmont College requires you to be around average in your high school class. You’ll need a mix of A’s and B’s, and very few C’s. If you have a lower GPA, you can compensate with harder courses like AP or IB classes.
