Is it legal to have same sex marriage in Louisiana?

Recognition of same-sex unions. Same-sex marriage is legal in Louisiana after the United States Supreme Court decision in Obergefell v. Hodges. Further, the ruling by the Louisiana Supreme Court in Costanza v. Caldwell made same-sex adoption along with same-sex marriage legal in Louisiana.
