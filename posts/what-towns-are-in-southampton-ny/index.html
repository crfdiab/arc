Where are the Hamptons located?

Long Island
The Hamptons are located on the South Fork of Long Island and are about two hours away from Midtown, Manhattan by car — if there’s no traffic, which there usually is. The area is made up of two proper towns: Southampton and East Hampton.

What is Southampton NY known for?

Southampton is the oldest and largest of communities in the summer colony known as The Hamptons. It is also arguably the commercial center of the southern “fork” of Long Island, serves as the home base for several region-wide businesses and has the area’s only hospital.
