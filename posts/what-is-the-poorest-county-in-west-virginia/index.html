Who founded Lewis County WV?

Charles Lewis
Lewis County, in north-central West Virginia (WV), was created in 1816 and named for Charles Lewis, a Virginian soldier and pioneer. Its county seat is at Weston. The West Fork River courses northward through the county from south to north.
