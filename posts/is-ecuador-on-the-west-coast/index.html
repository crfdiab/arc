Is Ecuador on the west coast?

ECUADOR IS A SMALL country located on the west coast of South America. The equator crosses the country, thus giving Ecuador its name. The country is bordered by the PACIFIC OCEAN to the west, COLOMBIA to the north, and PERU to the east and south.
