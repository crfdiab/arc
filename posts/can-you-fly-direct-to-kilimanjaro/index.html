Can you fly direct to Kilimanjaro?

The closest major cities to Kilimanjaro National Park are the city of Arusha and the town of Moshi. Depending on where you are traveling from, you can usually fly direct to Kilimanjaro Airport (JRO) via the Gulf (Qatar airlines, Emirates) or via Europe (KLM).

Which airlines fly into Kilimanjaro?

From Arusha, there are 4 airlines that have direct services to Kilimanjaro, which are Air Excel, Auric Air, Coastal Aviation and Regional Air. From Dar es Salaam, airlines that operate direct flights are Air Tanzania, KLM (SkyTeam), Precision Air, Qatar Airways (Oneworld), RwandAir and Utair Express.

When should I climb Kilimanjaro?

Guidebooks suggest climbing Kilimanjaro in January-February or August-September, when warmer temperatures and clear skies are most likely. But these are also the most crowded months. Climbing in March or October, just before each of two rainy seasons, is the best hedge to avoid both crowds and foul weather.
