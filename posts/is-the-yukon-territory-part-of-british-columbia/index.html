Is the Yukon Territory part of British Columbia?

Yukon Territory is one of the two Canadian territories bordering the Province of British Columbia. The territory and the province share a border to the north of British Columbia.

What is the border between Alberta and British Columbia?

The Alberta-British Columbia border features a number of physical features including Mount Assiniboine, Aurora Mountain, Mount Ball, Beatrice Peak, Bush Pass, and Popes Peak among other features. When British Columbia became a province in 1871, it had no money to conduct a boundary survey.

Which is the only Canadian province to border only one state?

What is the approximate shape of the Yukon Territory?
