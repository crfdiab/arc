How do I get to the financial district from Penn Station?

What subway do you take from Penn Station to SoHo?

To get from Penn Station to SoHo in Manhattan, take the A subway from 34 St-Penn Station station to W 4 St-Wash Sq station. Next, take the D subway from W 4 St-Wash Sq station to Broadway-Lafayette St [B,D,F,M,4,6] station. The total trip duration for this route is approximately 17 min. The ride fare is $2.75.

What subway lines go to Wall Street?
